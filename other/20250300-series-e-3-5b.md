# Anthropic E 轮：35亿美元

**时间：** 2025年3月  
**轮次：** E 轮  
**金额：** 35亿美元  
**领投：** Lightspeed Venture Partners  
**估值：** 615亿美元  
**ARR：** 约 10亿美元

---

## 概述

Anthropic 于 2025年3月完成 **E 轮** 融资 **35亿美元**。该轮由 **Lightspeed Venture Partners** 领投，公司估值 **615亿美元**。当时 Anthropic 的年 recurring 收入（ARR）约 **10亿美元**。

---

## 投资详情

| 指标 | 数值 |
|--------|-------|
| **轮次规模** | 35亿美元 |
| **领投** | Lightspeed Venture Partners |
| **估值** | 615亿美元 |
| **ARR** | 约 10亿美元 |

---

## 领投方

**Lightspeed Venture Partners** – 全球风险投资公司，投资包括 Snap、Epic Games、Carta 等科技公司。Lightspeed 在 AI 投资上活跃。

---

## 背景

- Claude 3.5 及后续模型已取得强劲市场 traction
- 企业采用显著加速
- 营收从约 9600万美元 ARR（2024年2月）增长至约 10亿美元 ARR（2025年3月）
- 与 OpenAI、Google 等的竞争依然激烈

---

## 估值增长

| 日期 | 估值 | 轮次 |
|------|-----------|-------|
| 2024年2月 | 177亿美元 | D 轮 |
| 2025年3月 | 615亿美元 | E 轮 |

估值在约一年内增长约 **3.5倍**。

---

## 资金用途

E 轮资金支持：

- 前沿模型开发（Claude 4 等）
- 大规模算力扩展
- 全球基础设施扩张
- 企业和 API 增长

# Anthropic Series E: $3.5 Billion

**Date:** March 2025  
**Round:** Series E  
**Amount:** $3.5 billion  
**Lead investor:** Lightspeed Venture Partners  
**Valuation:** $61.5 billion  
**ARR:** ~$1 billion

---

## Overview

Anthropic closed its **Series E** funding round of **$3.5 billion** in March 2025. The round was **led by Lightspeed Venture Partners**, valuing the company at **$61.5 billion**. At the time, Anthropic's annual recurring revenue (ARR) was approximately **$1 billion**.

---

## Investment Details

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Round size** | $3.5 billion |
| **Lead investor** | Lightspeed Venture Partners |
| **Valuation** | $61.5 billion |
| **ARR** | ~$1 billion |

---

## Lead Investor

**Lightspeed Venture Partners** – A global venture capital firm with investments in technology companies including Snap, Epic Games, and Carta. Lightspeed has been active in AI investments.

---

## Context

- Claude 3.5 and later models had achieved strong market traction
- Enterprise adoption had accelerated significantly
- Revenue had grown from ~$96M ARR (Feb 2024) to ~$1B ARR (Mar 2025)
- Competition with OpenAI, Google, and others remained intense

---

## Valuation Growth

| Date | Valuation | Round |
|------|-----------|-------|
| Feb 2024 | $17.7 billion | Series D |
| Mar 2025 | $61.5 billion | Series E |

The valuation increased approximately **3.5x** in about one year.

---

## Use of Funds

The Series E capital supported:

- Frontier model development (Claude 4, etc.)
- Massive compute scaling
- Global infrastructure expansion
- Enterprise and API growth
