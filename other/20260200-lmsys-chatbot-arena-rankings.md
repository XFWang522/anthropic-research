## 方法论

**LMSYS Chatbot Arena** 使用：

- **人类偏好投票** – 用户比较模型输出并选择偏好回复

- **众包评估** – 大规模、多样化用户群
- **分类排行榜** – 综合、编程、数学等

## Overview

In **February 2026**, the **LMSYS Chatbot Arena** released rankings in which **Claude Opus 4.5 (thinking)** ranked **#4 overall** with an Elo rating of **1466**. Claude **led the coding leaderboard** with an Elo of **1510**.

---

## Overall Rankings

| Rank | Model | Elo |
|------|-------|-----|
| 4 | **Claude Opus 4.5 (thinking)** | **1466** |

Claude Opus 4.5 with extended thinking ranked fourth overall among all evaluated models.

---

## Coding Leaderboard

| Rank | Model | Elo |
|------|-------|-----|
| 1 | **Claude** | **1510** |

Claude led the coding-specific leaderboard, reflecting strong performance on code generation, explanation, and debugging tasks.

---

## Methodology

**LMSYS Chatbot Arena** uses:

- **Human preference voting** – Users compare model outputs and choose preferred responses
- **Elo rating system** – Adapted from chess; models gain/lose points based on head-to-head results
- **Crowdsourced evaluation** – Large-scale, diverse user base
- **Category-specific leaderboards** – Overall, coding, math, etc.

---

## Context

- "Thinking" models use extended reasoning (chain-of-thought, scratchpad) before responding
- Claude Opus 4.5 thinking traded some speed for depth of reasoning
- Coding has been a key differentiator for Claude in enterprise and developer adoption
