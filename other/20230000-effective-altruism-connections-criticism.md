# 有效利他主义关联与批评

**时期：** 2023年起  
**主题：** Anthropic 与有效利他主义（EA）的关联及相应批评

---

## 概述

Anthropic 的创始人和早期团队与**有效利他主义（EA）**有联系，该运动专注于用证据和理性做最大的善。这些关联既获得支持也招致批评，包括**特朗普政府**的审查，以及将 EA 视为在 AI 政策中影响力过大的批评者的质疑。

---

## EA 关联

### Dario Amodei

- **Giving What We Can** – Dario Amodei 已签署 Giving What We Can 承诺，承诺将收入的相当一部分捐赠给有效慈善机构
- 长期参与 AI 安全和存在风险社区

### Daniela Amodei 与 Amanda Askell

- **淡化 EA 关联** – 在公开声明中，Daniela 和 Amanda 淡化或 nuanced 了 Anthropic 与 EA 的关联程度
- 试图将 Anthropic 的使命与 EA 正统区分开来

### EA Forum 批评

- **EA Forum** 及相关社区有时批评 Anthropic：
  - 商业关注优先于纯粹安全
  - 能力发展速度
  - 对安全承诺的 perceived（感知到的）妥协

---

## 批评与审查

### 特朗普政府

- **特朗普政府**审查了 Anthropic 的 EA 关联
- 担忧包括 AI 发展和政策倡导中潜在意识形态偏见
- 属于对"觉醒"或意识形态 aligned 科技公司更广泛审查的一部分（aligned 指立场一致）

### 一般批评

- **监管俘获** – 批评者认为 EA aligned 行为者在 AI 政策中影响力过大
- **与商业目标的张力** – Anthropic 的快速增长和商业化与 EA 对长期风险的强调形成对比
- **封闭性** – EA 社区有时被视为封闭或教条

---

## Anthropic 的定位

- Anthropic 强调其**安全优先使命**和 PBC 结构
- 公司试图吸引 EA 之外的广泛受众
- Constitutional AI 和 RSP 被定位为实用安全措施，而非纯 EA 项目

# Effective Altruism Connections and Criticism

**Period:** 2023 onward  
**Topic:** Anthropic's ties to Effective Altruism (EA) and associated criticism

---

## Overview

Anthropic's founders and early team have **connections to Effective Altruism (EA)**, a movement focused on using evidence and reason to do the most good. These ties have drawn both support and criticism, including scrutiny from the **Trump administration** and skepticism from critics who view EA as overly influential in AI policy.

---

## EA Connections

### Dario Amodei

- **Giving What We Can** – Dario Amodei has signed the Giving What We Can pledge, committing to donate a significant portion of income to effective charities
- Long-standing involvement in AI safety and existential risk communities

### Daniela Amodei and Amanda Askell

- **Downplayed EA ties** – In public statements, Daniela and Amanda have downplayed or nuanced the extent of Anthropic's EA connections
- Sought to distinguish Anthropic's mission from EA orthodoxy

### EA Forum Criticism

- The **EA Forum** and related communities have at times criticized Anthropic for:
  - Commercial focus over pure safety
  - Pace of capability development
  - Perceived compromises on safety commitments

---

## Criticism and Scrutiny

### Trump Administration

- The **Trump administration** scrutinized Anthropic's EA connections
- Concerns included potential ideological bias in AI development and policy advocacy
- Part of broader scrutiny of "woke" or ideologically aligned tech companies

### General Criticisms

- **Regulatory capture** – Critics argue EA-aligned actors have outsized influence on AI policy
- **Tension with commercial goals** – Anthropic's rapid growth and commercialization have been contrasted with EA's emphasis on long-term risk
- **Insularity** – EA communities are sometimes seen as insular or dogmatic

---

## Anthropic's Positioning

- Anthropic has emphasized its **safety-first mission** and PBC structure
- The company has sought to appeal to a broad audience beyond EA
- Constitutional AI and RSP are framed as practical safety measures, not purely EA projects
