## Overview

In **November 2025**, **Microsoft** invested **$5 billion** in Anthropic and committed **$30 billion** in Azure compute. **NVIDIA** invested **$10 billion**. The deals integrated Claude into **Microsoft Foundry**, **Microsoft 365 Copilot**, and **GitHub Copilot**.

---

## Microsoft Investment

| Component | Amount/Detail |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Equity investment** | $5 billion |
| **Azure compute commitment** | $30 billion |
| **Integration** | Claude in Foundry, M365 Copilot, GitHub Copilot |

---

## NVIDIA Investment

| Component | Amount |
|-----------|--------|
| **Equity investment** | $10 billion |

---

## Strategic Implications

### Microsoft

- **Multi-model strategy:** Microsoft already had a deep partnership with OpenAI; the Anthropic investment diversified its frontier AI portfolio
- **Enterprise AI:** Claude available via Foundry and M365 for enterprise customers
- **Developer tools:** GitHub Copilot could leverage Claude for code generation
- **Azure:** $30B compute commitment drove significant cloud revenue

### NVIDIA

- **AI infrastructure:** Anthropic is a major consumer of GPUs for training and inference
- **Strategic alignment:** Investment in a leading AI lab that relies on NVIDIA hardware
- **Ecosystem:** Strengthened relationship with a key customer and partner

### Anthropic

- **Multi-cloud:** Added Microsoft/Azure to AWS and Google Cloud
- **Distribution:** Access to Microsoft's enterprise and developer customer base
- **Compute:** $30B Azure commitment ensured long-term compute capacity

## Claude Integrations

- **Microsoft Foundry** – Enterprise AI platform
- **Microsoft 365 Copilot** – Productivity AI in Office apps
- **GitHub Copilot** – AI-assisted coding
