# FTX/SBF 投资争议

**时间：** 2022年4月（投资）；2022年11月起（争议）  
**主题：** FTX 和 Sam Bankman-Fried 对 Anthropic 的投资及后续影响

---

## 概述

**Sam Bankman-Fried（SBF）** 和 **FTX** 于 2022年4月领投 Anthropic 的 B 轮融资，Alameda Research 投资约 **5亿美元（$500 million）** 获得约 8% 股份。当 FTX 于 **2022年底** 在欺诈指控中崩盘时，该投资成为争议来源。该股份最终证明极具价值——在 Anthropic 1830亿美元估值（2025年9月）下约值 **146亿美元（$14.6 billion）**。

---

## 时间线

| 日期 | 事件 |
|------|-------|
| 2022年4月 | FTX/Alameda 投资约 5亿美元获得约 8%（B 轮） |
| 2022年11月 | FTX 崩盘；SBF 被捕；申请破产 |
| 2023–2024年 | FTX 破产财团保留 Anthropic 股份 |
| 2024年3月 | 财团以 8.84亿美元出售约 2/3 股份 |
| 2024年6月 | 财团以约 4.5亿美元出售剩余股份；总回收约 13亿美元 |
| 2025年9月 | Anthropic 估值 1830亿美元；原股份本可值约 146亿美元 |

---

## 争议要素

### 资金来源

- FTX 客户资金据称被滥用；Anthropic 投资可能由混合或挪用的资金资助
- 破产程序寻求为债权人追回资产

### SBF 的动机

- SBF 是知名有效利他主义者；AI 安全是其 stated 优先事项
- 批评者质疑该投资是 genuine 慈善还是声誉建设
- 投资的成功成为 EA 和 AI 安全圈子的谈资

### Anthropic 的关联

- Anthropic 面临关于其与 FTX 和 SBF 关联的质疑
- 公司强调其未参与 FTX 运营或 alleged 欺诈
- 股份由破产财团持有，而非 Anthropic

---

## 财务结果

- **原始投资：** 约 5亿美元  
- **财团回收：** 约 13亿美元（来自 2024年出售）  
- **峰值价值（若持有至 2025年9月）：** 在 1830亿美元估值下约 146亿美元  

财团于 2024年出售；若持有，股份价值将远高于此。出售为债权人提供了 meaningful 回收。

# FTX/SBF Investment Controversy

**Date:** April 2022 (investment); November 2022 onward (controversy)  
**Topic:** FTX and Sam Bankman-Fried's investment in Anthropic and subsequent fallout

---

## Overview

**Sam Bankman-Fried (SBF)** and **FTX** led Anthropic's Series B in April 2022, with Alameda Research investing approximately **$500 million** for ~8% of the company. When FTX collapsed in **late 2022** amid fraud allegations, the investment became a source of controversy. The stake eventually proved extremely valuable—worth approximately **$14.6 billion** at Anthropic's $183 billion valuation (September 2025).

---

## Timeline

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| April 2022 | FTX/Alameda invests ~$500M for ~8% (Series B) |
| November 2022 | FTX collapses; SBF arrested; bankruptcy filed |
| 2023–2024 | FTX estate retains Anthropic stake |
| March 2024 | Estate sells ~2/3 of stake for $884M |
| June 2024 | Estate sells remaining stake for ~$450M; total ~$1.3B recovery |
| September 2025 | Anthropic valued at $183B; original stake would have been ~$14.6B |

---

## Controversy Elements

### Source of Funds

- FTX customer funds were allegedly misused; the Anthropic investment may have been funded with commingled or misappropriated funds
- Bankruptcy proceedings sought to recover assets for creditors

### SBF's Motives

- SBF was a prominent effective altruist; AI safety was a stated priority
- Critics questioned whether the investment was genuine philanthropy or reputation-building
- The investment's success became a talking point in EA and AI safety circles

### Anthropic's Association

- Anthropic faced questions about its association with FTX and SBF
- The company emphasized it had no involvement in FTX's operations or alleged fraud
- The stake was held by the bankruptcy estate, not by Anthropic

---

## Financial Outcome

- **Original investment:** ~$500 million  
- **Estate recovery:** ~$1.3 billion (from sales in 2024)  
- **Peak value (if held to Sep 2025):** ~$14.6 billion at $183B valuation  

The estate sold in 2024; had it held, the stake would have been worth far more. The sales provided meaningful recovery for creditors.
