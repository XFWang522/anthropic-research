# 政治捐款：向 Public First Action 捐款 2000万美元

**时间：** 2026年2月  
**金额：** 2000万美元  
**接收方：** Public First Action（超级 PAC）  
**目的：** AI 监管及支持性候选人

---

## 概述

2026年2月，Anthropic 向 **Public First Action** 捐款 **2000万美元**，这是一个支持 **AI 监管**相关候选人和事业的超级 PAC。该捐款与 **OpenAI 支持的 Leading the Future** 形成对比，后者承诺 **1.25亿美元** 政治支出。

---

## 捐款详情

| 指标 | 数值 |
|--------|-------|
| **金额** | 2000万美元 |
| **接收方** | Public First Action（超级 PAC） |
| **重点** | AI 监管、支持性候选人 |

---

## Public First Action

**Public First Action** 是一个超级 PAC，其特点为：

- 支持赞成 AI 监管的候选人
- 倡导 AI 安全与治理的政策立场
- 参与联邦和州选举

---

## 对比：Anthropic 与 OpenAI

| 公司 | 金额 | 接收方 |
|---------|--------|-----------|
| **Anthropic** | 2000万美元 | Public First Action |
| **OpenAI** | 1.25亿美元 | Leading the Future |

OpenAI 的 **Leading the Future** 超级 PAC 承诺 **1.25亿美元**，显著高于 Anthropic 的 2000万美元。两家公司都日益参与政治支出以塑造 AI 政策。

---

## 背景

- AI 监管是 2025–2026年主要政策辩论
- 科技公司大量投资游说和政治竞选
- Anthropic 的捐款反映其塑造监管结果的兴趣
- 特朗普政府对 Anthropic 的审查（参见 [20260200-pentagon-contract-dispute.md](20260200-pentagon-contract-dispute.md)）可能影响了政治策略

# Political Donation: $20 Million to Public First Action

**Date:** February 2026

**Recipient:** Public First Action (super PAC)

---

## Overview

In **February 2026**, Anthropic contributed **$20 million** to **Public First Action**, a super PAC supporting candidates and causes aligned with **AI regulation**. The donation contrasted with **OpenAI-backed Leading the Future**, which had committed **$125 million** to political spending.

---

## Donation Details

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Amount** | $20 million |
| **Recipient** | Public First Action (super PAC) |
| **Focus** | AI regulation, supportive candidates |

---

## Public First Action

**Public First Action** is a super PAC that:

- Supports candidates who favor AI regulation
- Advocates for policy positions on AI safety and governance
- Engages in federal and state elections

---

## Comparison: Anthropic vs. OpenAI

| Company | Amount | Recipient |
|---------|--------|-----------|
| **Anthropic** | $20 million | Public First Action |
| **OpenAI** | $125 million | Leading the Future |

OpenAI's **Leading the Future** super PAC had committed **$125 million**, significantly more than Anthropic's $20 million. Both companies were increasingly engaged in political spending to shape AI policy.

## Context

- AI regulation was a major policy debate in 2025–2026
- Tech companies were investing heavily in lobbying and political campaigns
- Anthropic's donation reflected its interest in shaping regulatory outcomes
