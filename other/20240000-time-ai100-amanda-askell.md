## Constitutional AI

**Constitutional AI** 是 Anthropic 开发的一种训练方法，其特点为：

- 使用原则"宪法"指导模型行为
- 通过自我批评和强化减少有害输出
- 提升 helpfulness、harmlessness 和 honesty（HHH）
- 已应用于 Claude 及其他 Anthropic 模型

## Overview

**Amanda Askell**, co-founder and researcher at Anthropic, was named to **TIME's AI 100** list in **2024** for her work on **Constitutional AI**, a training methodology that embeds values and constraints into AI systems to improve safety and alignment.

---

## Amanda Askell

- **Role:** Co-founder and researcher at Anthropic
- **Background:** Former researcher at OpenAI; left in late 2020/early 2021
- **Focus:** Constitutional AI, AI safety, alignment
- **Recognition:** TIME AI 100 (2024)

---

## Constitutional AI

**Constitutional AI** is a training approach developed at Anthropic that:

- Uses a "constitution" of principles to guide model behavior
- Reduces harmful outputs through self-critique and reinforcement
- Improves helpfulness, harmlessness, and honesty (HHH)
- Has been applied to Claude and other Anthropic models

**TIME AI 100** is a list recognizing 100 influential figures in artificial intelligence, including researchers, executives, policymakers, and advocates. The list highlights individuals shaping the development and impact of AI.
