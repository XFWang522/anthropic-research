# Anthropic C 轮：4.5亿美元

**时间：** 2023年5月  
**轮次：** C 轮  
**金额：** 4.5亿美元

---

## 概述

Anthropic 于 2023年5月完成 **C 轮** 融资 **4.5亿美元**。该轮由 **Spark Capital** 领投，**Google** 作为现有投资者参与。

---

## 领投方

**Spark Capital** – 以投资科技和消费公司闻名的风险投资公司，包括 Twitter、Slack、Discord。

---

## 主要投资者

| 投资者 | 角色 |
|----------|------|
| **Spark Capital** | 领投 |
| **Google** | 参与（2023年2月现有投资者） |

---

## 背景

- Claude 已于 2023年初公开发布（Claude 1）
- 与 OpenAI（GPT-4）及其他实验室的竞争加剧
- Anthropic 需要资金扩展训练、推理和企业采用
- Google 的参与强化了云合作

---

## 资金用途

C 轮资金支持：

- Claude 模型开发的扩展
- API 和产品线的扩张
- 企业销售和支持
- 持续安全研究

---

## 估值

C 轮估值未完全披露，但公司估值较 2023年2月 Google 投资 implied 的约 50亿美元显著上升。

# Anthropic Series C: $450 Million

**Date:** May 2023  
**Round:** Series C  
**Amount:** $450 million

---

## Overview

Anthropic closed its **Series C** funding round of **$450 million** in May 2023. The round was **led by Spark Capital**, with **Google** participating as an existing investor.

---

## Lead Investor

**Spark Capital** – A venture capital firm known for investments in technology and consumer companies, including Twitter, Slack, and Discord.

---

## Key Investors

| Investor | Role |
|----------|------|
| **Spark Capital** | Lead investor |
| **Google** | Participating (existing investor from Feb 2023) |

---

## Context

- Claude had been released publicly (Claude 1) in early 2023
- Competition with OpenAI (GPT-4) and other labs was intensifying
- Anthropic needed capital to scale training, inference, and enterprise adoption
- Google's participation reinforced the cloud partnership

---

## Use of Funds

The Series C capital supported:

- Scaling of Claude model development
- Expansion of API and product offerings
- Enterprise sales and support
- Continued safety research

---

## Valuation

Valuation for the Series C round was not fully disclosed, but the company's valuation had increased significantly from the ~$5B implied by the February 2023 Google investment.
