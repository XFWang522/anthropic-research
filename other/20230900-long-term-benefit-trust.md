# 长期利益信托（LTBT）

**时间：** 2023年9月  
**主题：** Anthropic 的长期利益信托治理结构

---

## 概述

2023年9月，Anthropic 设立了**长期利益信托（LTBT）**，这是一个由**五名无利害关系成员**组成的治理机制，他们可以**遴选和罢免董事会成员**。LTBT 通过确保在公司治理中考虑长期社会利益，支持公司的**公益公司（PBC）**定位。

---

## 结构

| 要素 | 详情 |
|---------|--------|
| **成员** | 五名无利害关系成员 |
| **权力** | 遴选和罢免董事会成员 |
| **目的** | 使治理与长期利益保持一致 |
| **与 PBC 的关系** | 支持 PBC 使命和问责 |

---

## 无利害关系成员

LTBT 由**五名无利害关系成员**组成——即非员工、非投资者或与 Anthropic 无财务关联的个人。该结构旨在：

- 减少利益冲突
- 确保治理考虑更广泛的社会影响
- 对短期商业压力形成制衡

---

## 董事会遴选与罢免

LTBT 有权：

- **遴选**董事会成员
- **罢免**董事会成员

这使信托对 Anthropic 的领导层和战略方向具有重大影响力。

---

## PBC 定位

Anthropic 是一家**特拉华州公益公司**，要求公司在股东利益与指定公共利益之间取得平衡。LTBT 通过以下方式强化这一点：

- 将长期思维嵌入治理
- 提供对社会利益问责的机制
- 将 Anthropic 与传统营利性公司区分开来

# Long-Term Benefit Trust (LTBT)

**Date:** September 2023  
**Topic:** Anthropic's Long-Term Benefit Trust governance structure

---

## Overview

In **September 2023**, Anthropic established the **Long-Term Benefit Trust (LTBT)**, a governance mechanism with **five disinterested members** who can **select and remove board members**. The LTBT supports the company's **Public Benefit Corporation (PBC)** alignment by ensuring that long-term societal benefit is considered in corporate governance.

---

## Structure

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Members** | Five disinterested members |
| **Powers** | Select and remove board members |
| **Purpose** | Align governance with long-term benefit |
| **Relation to PBC** | Supports PBC mission and accountability |

---

## Disinterested Members

The LTBT is composed of **five disinterested members**—individuals who are not employees, investors, or otherwise financially tied to Anthropic. This structure is intended to:

- Reduce conflicts of interest
- Ensure governance considers broader societal impact
- Provide a check on short-term commercial pressure

---

## Board Selection and Removal

The LTBT has the power to:

- **Select** board members
- **Remove** board members

This gives the trust significant influence over Anthropic's leadership and strategic direction.

---

## PBC Alignment

Anthropic is a **Delaware Public Benefit Corporation**, which requires the company to balance stockholder interests with specified public benefits. The LTBT reinforces this by:

- Embedding long-term thinking in governance
- Providing a mechanism for accountability to societal benefit
- Differentiating Anthropic from conventional for-profit corporations
