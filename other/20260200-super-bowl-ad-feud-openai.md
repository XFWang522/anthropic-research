# 超级碗广告与 OpenAI 争执

**时间：** 2026年2月（超级碗 LIX）  
**主题：** Anthropic 超级碗广告批评 OpenAI 的 ChatGPT 广告

---

## 概述

在 **2026年2月** 的**超级碗 LIX**期间，Anthropic 投放了一系列**四支电视广告**，含蓄批评 **OpenAI 的 ChatGPT 广告**。广告强调**"广告正在进入 AI。但 Claude 不会。"** **Sam Altman**（OpenAI CEO）称这些广告**"不诚实"**。

---

## Anthropic 的广告

Anthropic 投放**四支广告**，每支以单一主题词为核心：

| 广告 | 主题 |
|------|-------|
| 1 | **背叛** |
| 2 | **欺骗** |
| 3 | **背信** |
| 4 | **侵犯** |

广告暗示 AI 助手将很快向用户展示广告，这代表对用户信任的背叛。Claude 被定位为不会展示广告的替代选择。

---

## 核心信息

**"广告正在进入 AI。但 Claude 不会。"**

- Anthropic 将广告支持的 AI 描绘为负面发展
- 暗示 OpenAI/ChatGPT 正在或将要引入广告
- 将 Claude 定位为更以用户为中心、无广告

---

## Sam Altman 的回应

**Sam Altman**（OpenAI CEO）公开称这些广告**"不诚实"**，暗示：

- 对 OpenAI 计划的描述不准确
- 广告歪曲了竞争格局
- 信息对消费者具有误导性

---

## 背景

- 超级碗广告昂贵且曝光度高；争执引发大量媒体关注
- AI 助手市场竞争激烈；在信任和用户体验上的差异化具有战略意义
- 关于广告支持 vs 订阅 AI 模型的辩论持续进行

# Super Bowl Ad Feud with OpenAI

**Date:** February 2026 (Super Bowl LIX)  
**Topic:** Anthropic's Super Bowl ads criticizing OpenAI's ChatGPT ads

---

## Overview

During **Super Bowl LIX** in **February 2026**, Anthropic ran a series of **four television ads** that implicitly criticized **OpenAI's ChatGPT ads**. The ads emphasized that **"Ads are coming to AI. But not to Claude."** **Sam Altman** (OpenAI CEO) called the ads **"dishonest."**

---

## Anthropic's Ads

Anthropic ran **four spots**, each with a single-word theme:

| Spot | Theme |
|------|-------|
| 1 | **Betrayal** |
| 2 | **Deception** |
| 3 | **Treachery** |
| 4 | **Violation** |

The ads suggested that AI assistants would soon show ads to users, and that this represented a betrayal of user trust. Claude was positioned as the alternative that would not show ads.

---

## Key Message

**"Ads are coming to AI. But not to Claude."**

- Anthropic framed ad-supported AI as a negative development
- Implied that OpenAI/ChatGPT was or would be introducing ads
- Positioned Claude as more user-aligned and ad-free

---

## Sam Altman's Response

**Sam Altman** (OpenAI CEO) publicly called the ads **"dishonest,"** suggesting that:

- The characterization of OpenAI's plans was inaccurate
- The ads misrepresented the competitive landscape
- The messaging was misleading to consumers

---

## Context

- Super Bowl ads are expensive and high-visibility; the feud drew significant media attention
- The AI assistant market was highly competitive; differentiation on trust and user experience was strategic
- Debate over ad-supported vs. subscription AI models was ongoing
