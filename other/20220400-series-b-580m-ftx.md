# Anthropic B 轮：5.8亿美元（FTX 领投）

**时间：** 2022年4月  
**轮次：** B 轮  
**金额：** 5.8亿美元

---

## 概述

Anthropic 于 2022年4月完成 **B 轮** 融资 **5.8亿美元**。该轮由 **Sam Bankman-Fried（SBF）** 和 **FTX** 领投，Alameda Research 投资约 **5亿美元** 获得约 **8%** 股份。

---

## 领投方

**Sam Bankman-Fried / FTX** – 当时 SBF 是 FTX（主要加密货币交易所）和 Alameda Research（量化交易公司）的创始人。他也是知名有效利他主义者，曾表示支持 AI 安全。

---

## 投资结构

| 实体 | 投资 | 股份 |
|--------|------------|-------|
| **Alameda Research** | 约 5亿美元 | 约 8% |
| **其他投资者** | 约 8000万美元 | — |

---

## 背景

- FTX 和 Alameda 在 2022年初处于估值巅峰
- SBF 曾公开倡导"earning to give"和支持高影响力事业
- AI 安全是有效利他主义资助的重点领域
- 该投资被视为将 SBF 财富与长期 AI 风险缓解 alignment

---

## 后续发展

2022年11月，FTX 在欺诈指控中崩盘。Anthropic 股份成为 FTX 破产财团最有价值的资产之一。财团最终分多批出售股份（参见 [20240300-ftx-sells-stake-884m.md](20240300-ftx-sells-stake-884m.md) 和 [20240600-ftx-sells-remaining-shares-450m.md](20240600-ftx-sells-remaining-shares-450m.md)）。

# Anthropic Series B: $580 Million (FTX-Led)

**Date:** April 2022  
**Round:** Series B  
**Amount:** $580 million

---

## Overview

Anthropic closed its **Series B** funding round of **$580 million** in April 2022. The round was **led by Sam Bankman-Fried (SBF)** and **FTX**, with Alameda Research investing approximately **$500 million** for roughly **8%** of the company.

---

## Lead Investor

**Sam Bankman-Fried / FTX** – At the time, SBF was the founder of FTX (a major cryptocurrency exchange) and Alameda Research (a quantitative trading firm). He was also a prominent effective altruist and had expressed interest in supporting AI safety.

---

## Investment Structure

| Entity | Investment | Stake |
|--------|------------|-------|
| **Alameda Research** | ~$500 million | ~8% |
| **Other investors** | ~$80 million | — |

---

## Context

- FTX and Alameda were at peak valuation in early 2022
- SBF had publicly advocated for "earning to give" and supporting high-impact causes
- AI safety was a focus area for effective altruism funding
- The investment was seen as aligning SBF's wealth with long-term AI risk mitigation

---

## Later Developments

In **November 2022**, FTX collapsed amid fraud allegations. The Anthropic stake became one of the most valuable assets in the FTX bankruptcy estate. The estate eventually sold the stake in multiple tranches (see [20240300-ftx-sells-stake-884m.md](20240300-ftx-sells-stake-884m.md) and [20240600-ftx-sells-remaining-shares-450m.md](20240600-ftx-sells-remaining-shares-450m.md)).
