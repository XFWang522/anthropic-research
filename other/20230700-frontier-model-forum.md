## 使命与活动

前沿模型论坛旨在：

- **推进 AI 安全研究** – 支持前沿模型安全研究
- **确立最佳实践** – 制定和推广安全实践

# Frontier Model Forum

**Date:** July 2023  
**Founders:** Anthropic, Google, Microsoft, OpenAI  
**Later members:** Amazon, Meta (May 2024)

---

## Overview

In **July 2023**, **Anthropic**, **Google**, **Microsoft**, and **OpenAI** launched the **Frontier Model Forum**, an industry initiative focused on the safe development and deployment of frontier AI models. **Amazon** and **Meta** joined in **May 2024**.

---

---

## Later Members

| Company | Join date |
|---------|-----------|
| **Amazon** | May 2024 |
| **Meta** | May 2024 |

---

## Mission and Activities

The Frontier Model Forum aims to:

- **Advance AI safety research** – Support research on frontier model safety
- **Identify best practices** – Develop and promote safety practices
- **Collaborate with policymakers** – Engage with governments and regulators
- **Support information sharing** – Facilitate coordination among frontier labs

---

## Context

- The Forum was launched amid growing regulatory and public concern about AI risks
- It represented an effort by leading labs to self-regulate and engage with policy
- Critics have questioned whether industry-led initiatives can adequately address risks
