# FTX 出售剩余 Anthropic 股份：约 4.5亿美元

**时间：** 2024年6月  
**金额：** 约 4.5亿美元  
**FTX 财团总回收：** 约 13亿美元

---

## 概述

2024年6月，**FTX 破产财团**以约 **4.5亿美元** 出售其 **剩余** Anthropic 股份。加上 2024年3月以 **8.84亿美元** 出售约 2/3 股份，财团从其 Anthropic 投资中回收约 **13亿美元**。

---

## 出售时间线

| 日期 | 金额 | 股份 |
|------|--------|-------|
| 2024年3月 | 8.84亿美元 | 约 2/3 持股 |
| 2024年6月 | 约 4.5亿美元 | 剩余约 1/3 |
| **总计** | **约 13亿美元** | 100% |

---

## 原始投资 vs 回收

| 指标 | 数值 |
|--------|-------|
| **原始投资（2022年4月）** | 约 5亿美元（Alameda） |
| **总回收** | 约 13亿美元 |
| **约回报倍数** | 约 2.6倍 |

---

## 背景

- Anthropic 估值在 2024年持续上升
- 财团优先考虑流动性以资助债权人分配
- 剩余股份可能出售给机构投资者或二级买家

---

## 对 FTX 债权人的影响

Anthropic 投资是 FTX 财团最成功的资产之一。约 13亿美元回收对债权人偿付有 meaningful 贡献，尽管财团面临欺诈和管理不善造成的重大缺口。

# FTX Sells Remaining Anthropic Shares: ~$450 Million

**Date:** June 2024  
**Amount:** ~$450 million  
**Total FTX estate recovery:** ~$1.3 billion

---

## Overview

In **June 2024**, the **FTX bankruptcy estate** sold its **remaining** Anthropic shares for approximately **$450 million**. Combined with the March 2024 sale of ~2/3 of the stake for **$884 million**, the estate recovered approximately **$1.3 billion** from its Anthropic investment.

---

## Sale Timeline

| Date | Amount | Stake |
|------|--------|-------|
| March 2024 | $884 million | ~2/3 of holdings |
| June 2024 | ~$450 million | Remaining ~1/3 |
| **Total** | **~$1.3 billion** | 100% |

---

## Original Investment vs. Recovery

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Original investment (Apr 2022)** | ~$500 million (Alameda) |
| **Total recovery** | ~$1.3 billion |
| **Approximate return** | ~2.6x |

---

## Context

- Anthropic's valuation continued to rise through 2024
- The estate prioritized liquidity to fund creditor distributions
- The remaining stake was likely sold to institutional investors or secondary buyers

---

## Implications for FTX Creditors

The Anthropic investment was one of the most successful assets in the FTX estate. The ~$1.3 billion recovery contributed meaningfully to creditor repayments, though the estate faced significant shortfalls from fraud and mismanagement.
