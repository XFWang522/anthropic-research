# RSP v3.0 发布

**时间：** 2026年2月  
**主题：** 负责任扩展政策 v3.0 技术发布

---

## 概述

2026年2月，Anthropic 发布了**负责任扩展政策（RSP）v3.0**，这是其安全与扩展框架的重大更新。新版本引入了**前沿安全路线图**、每3–6个月发布的**风险报告**，以及更清晰的**单边承诺与行业承诺的区分**。

---

## RSP v3.0 的主要变化

### 前沿安全路线图

- 用于规划和沟通安全措施的新结构
- 路线图概述 Anthropic 将如何在不同能力层级应对风险
- 比以往版本更细粒度、更具前瞻性

### 风险报告

- **频率：** 每3–6个月
- **内容：** 模型能力、风险和缓解措施的评估
- **透明度：** 安全态势的公开报告

### 单边承诺与行业承诺

RSP v3.0 **区分了**：

- **单边承诺** – Anthropic 承诺独立完成的事项，无论行业如何
- **行业承诺** – Anthropic 倡导作为行业标准或监管的事项

这一区分明确了哪些承诺对 Anthropic 具有约束力，哪些是更广泛行业的愿景。

---

## 技术细节

- 更新了 ASL（AI 安全等级）定义和阈值
- 修订了评估方法
- 新的治理和问责机制

---

## 反响

- **批评者**认为更新削弱了承诺（参见 [20260200-rsp-overhaul-safety-criticism.md](20260200-rsp-overhaul-safety-criticism.md)）
- **Anthropic** 将其定位为更实用、更灵活的方法

# RSP v3.0 Release

**Date:** February 2026  
**Topic:** Responsible Scaling Policy v3.0 technical release

---

## Overview

In **February 2026**, Anthropic released **Responsible Scaling Policy (RSP) v3.0**, a major update to its safety and scaling framework. The new version introduced **Frontier Safety Roadmaps**, **Risk Reports** every 3–6 months, and a clearer **separation between unilateral and industry-wide commitments**.

---

## Key Changes in RSP v3.0

### Frontier Safety Roadmaps

- New structure for planning and communicating safety measures
- Roadmaps outline how Anthropic will address risks at different capability levels
- More granular and forward-looking than previous versions

### Risk Reports

- **Frequency:** Every 3–6 months
- **Content:** Assessment of model capabilities, risks, and mitigation measures
- **Transparency:** Public reporting on safety posture

### Unilateral vs. Industry-Wide

RSP v3.0 **separated**:

- **Unilateral commitments** – What Anthropic commits to do on its own, regardless of industry
- **Industry-wide** – What Anthropic advocates for as industry standards or regulation

This distinction clarified which commitments were binding on Anthropic vs. aspirational for the broader industry.

---

## Technical Details

- Updated ASL (AI Safety Level) definitions and thresholds
- Revised evaluation methodologies
- New governance and accountability mechanisms

---

## Reception

- **Critics** argued the update weakened commitments (see [20260200-rsp-overhaul-safety-criticism.md](20260200-rsp-overhaul-safety-criticism.md))
- **Anthropic** framed it as a more practical, adaptable approach
- **EA Forum** described it as "quietly backpedalling"
