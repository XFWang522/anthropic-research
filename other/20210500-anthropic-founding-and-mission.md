# Anthropic 创立与使命

**时间：** 2021年5月  
**主题：** Anthropic 作为特拉华州公益公司正式创立

---

## 概述

Anthropic 于 **2021年5月** 正式创立，为总部位于旧金山的**特拉华州公益公司（PBC）**。公司由八位此前离开 OpenAI 的联合创始人创立，初期 focus 为 AI 安全研究。

---

## 公司结构

### 公益公司

Anthropic 选择**特拉华州 PBC** 结构，要求公司在股东利益与一项或多项指定公共利益之间取得平衡。这在法律上将对社会利益的承诺与商业目标并置。

### 地点

- **总部：** 加利福尼亚州旧金山
- **注册地：** 特拉华州

---

## 创始团队（八位联合创始人）

八位联合创始人包括：

1. **Dario Amodei** – CEO（前 OpenAI 研究副总裁）
2. **Daniela Amodei** – 总裁（运营与领导）
3. **Tom Brown** – 研究（OpenAI GPT-3 负责人）
4. **Chris Olah** – 可解释性与安全研究
5. **Amanda Askell** – 研究（Constitutional AI）
6. **Jared Kaplan** – 研究
7. **Jack Clark** – 政策与传播
8. **Ben Mann** – 研究

---

## 使命陈述

Anthropic 的 stated 使命是构建**"可 steerable、可 interpretable、且 robust 的大规模 AI 系统"**。

### 核心支柱

| 支柱 | 描述 |
|--------|-------------|
| **可 steerable** | 可被引导和控制以与用户意图和价值观 alignment 的 AI 系统 |
| **可 interpretable** | 行为和推理可被理解和解释的系统 |
| **Robust** | 在各种条件和 edge cases 下可靠、安全运行的系统 |

---

## 初期 focus

公司的**初期 focus** 为：

- **安全研究** – 开发使 AI 系统更安全、更 alignment 的技术
- **可解释性** – 理解模型如何做决策
- **Constitutional AI** – 将价值观和约束嵌入模型行为的训练方法
- **扩展定律与能力** – 理解能力与风险如何随模型规模扩展

# Anthropic Founding and Mission

**Date:** May 2021  
**Topic:** Formal founding of Anthropic as a Delaware Public Benefit Corporation

---

## Overview

Anthropic was formally founded in **May 2021** as a **Delaware Public Benefit Corporation (PBC)** headquartered in San Francisco. The company was established by eight co-founders who had previously left OpenAI, with an initial focus on AI safety research.

---

## Corporate Structure

### Public Benefit Corporation

Anthropic chose the **Delaware PBC** structure, which requires the corporation to balance stockholder interests with one or more specific public benefits. This legally embeds a commitment to societal good alongside commercial objectives.

### Location

- **Headquarters:** San Francisco, California
- **Incorporation:** Delaware

---

## Founding Team (Eight Co-Founders)

The eight co-founders included:

1. **Dario Amodei** – CEO (formerly VP of Research at OpenAI)
2. **Daniela Amodei** – President (operations and leadership)
3. **Tom Brown** – Research (GPT-3 lead at OpenAI)
4. **Chris Olah** – Interpretability and safety research
5. **Amanda Askell** – Research (Constitutional AI)
6. **Jared Kaplan** – Research
7. **Jack Clark** – Policy and communications
8. **Ben Mann** – Research

---

## Mission Statement

Anthropic's stated mission is to build **"large-scale AI systems that are steerable, interpretable, and robust."**

### Core Pillars

| Pillar | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Steerable** | AI systems that can be directed and controlled to align with user intent and values |
| **Interpretable** | Systems whose behavior and reasoning can be understood and explained |
| **Robust** | Systems that behave reliably and safely across diverse conditions and edge cases |

---

## Initial Focus

The company's **initial focus** was on:

- **Safety research** – Developing techniques to make AI systems safer and more aligned
- **Interpretability** – Understanding how models make decisions
- **Constitutional AI** – Training methods that embed values and constraints into model behavior
- **Scaling laws and capabilities** – Understanding how capabilities and risks scale with model size
