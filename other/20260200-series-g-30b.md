## 概述

Anthropic 于 2026年2月完成 **G 轮** 融资 **300亿美元**，公司估值 **3800亿美元**。主要投资者包括 **GIC**（新加坡主权财富基金）和 **Coatue**。当时 Anthropic 的年 recurring 收入为 **140亿美元**。

## Overview

Anthropic closed its **Series G** funding round of **$30 billion** in February 2026, valuing the company at **$380 billion**. Key investors included **GIC** (Singapore's sovereign wealth fund) and **Coatue**. At the time, Anthropic's annual recurring revenue was **$14 billion**.

---

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Round size** | $30 billion |
| **Valuation** | $380 billion |
| **Key investors** | GIC, Coatue |
| **Revenue (ARR)** | $14 billion |

---

## Key Investors

| Investor | Profile |
|----------|---------|
| **GIC** | Government of Singapore Investment Corporation; one of the world's largest sovereign wealth funds |
| **Coatue** | Technology-focused hedge fund and growth investor |

---

## Valuation Growth

| Date | Valuation | Round |
|------|-----------|-------|
| Sep 2025 | $183 billion | Series F |
| Feb 2026 | $380 billion | Series G |

The valuation increased approximately **2x** in five months.

---

## Revenue Context

At **$14 billion ARR**, Anthropic had become one of the largest AI companies by revenue. The $380 billion valuation implied a revenue multiple of approximately **27x ARR**.

---

## Context

- Anthropic had established itself as a leading frontier AI company
- Competition with OpenAI, Google, and others remained intense
- Sovereign wealth funds and large asset managers were increasingly allocating to AI
- Regulatory and policy developments continued to shape the industry
