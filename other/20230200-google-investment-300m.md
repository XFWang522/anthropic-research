# Google 投资：3亿美元

**时间：** 2023年2月  
**金额：** 3亿美元  
**股份：** 约 10%  
**估值：** 约 50亿美元

---

## 概述

2023年2月，**Google** 向 Anthropic 投资 **3亿美元**，获得约 **10%** 股份， implied 估值约 **50亿美元**。该投资同时确立 **Google Cloud** 为 Anthropic 的主要云基础设施提供商。

---

## 投资详情

| 指标 | 数值 |
|--------|-------|
| **投资** | 3亿美元 |
| **股份** | 约 10% |
| **推算估值** | 约 50亿美元 |
| **云提供商** | Google Cloud（GPU 和 TPU） |

---

## 战略考量

### 对 Anthropic

- 获得 **Google Cloud** 算力（GPU 和 TPU）用于训练和推理
- 来自主要科技公司的 validation
- 扩展研究和产品开发的资金

### 对 Google

- 与领先 AI 安全实验室合作
- Claude 集成到 Google Cloud 生态
- 前沿 AI 战略定位

---

## 云合作

该投资与 Anthropic 使用 **Google Cloud** 作为其主要云提供商挂钩，包括：

- **GPU** – 用于模型训练和推理
- **TPU** – Google 定制 AI 加速器

# Google Investment: $300 Million

**Date:** February 2023  
**Amount:** $300 million  
**Stake:** ~10%  
**Valuation:** ~$5 billion

---

## Overview

In **February 2023**, **Google** invested **$300 million** in Anthropic for approximately **10%** of the company, implying a valuation of roughly **$5 billion**. The investment also established **Google Cloud** as Anthropic's primary cloud infrastructure provider.

---

## Investment Details

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Investment** | $300 million |
| **Stake** | ~10% |
| **Implied valuation** | ~$5 billion |
| **Cloud provider** | Google Cloud (GPUs and TPUs) |

---

## Strategic Rationale

### For Anthropic

- Access to **Google Cloud** compute (GPUs and TPUs) for training and inference
- Validation from a major technology company
- Capital to scale research and product development

### For Google

- Partnership with a leading AI safety-focused lab
- Integration of Claude into Google Cloud ecosystem
- Strategic positioning in frontier AI

---

## Cloud Partnership

The investment was tied to Anthropic's use of **Google Cloud** as its primary cloud provider, including:

- **GPUs** – For model training and inference
- **TPUs** – Google's custom AI accelerators

---

## Later Google Investments

Google subsequently made additional investments:

- **October 2023:** Additional **$2 billion** commitment (total $2.3 billion) – see [20231000-google-additional-2b.md](20231000-google-additional-2b.md)
