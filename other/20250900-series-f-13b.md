## Overview

Anthropic closed its **Series F** funding round of **$13 billion** in September 2025, valuing the company at **$183 billion**. The round included **ICONIQ**, **Fidelity**, and **Lightspeed** as key investors.

---

## Investment Details

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Round size** | $13 billion |
| **Valuation** | $183 billion |
| **Key investors** | ICONIQ, Fidelity, Lightspeed |

---

## Key Investors

| Investor | Profile |
|----------|---------|
| **ICONIQ** | Wealth management and growth investing; clients include tech executives |
| **Fidelity** | Major asset manager and mutual fund provider |
| **Lightspeed** | Lead investor in Series E; continued participation |

---

## Valuation Growth

| Date | Valuation | Round |
|------|-----------|-------|
| Mar 2025 | $61.5 billion | Series E |
| Sep 2025 | $183 billion | Series F |

The valuation increased approximately **3x** in six months.

---

## Context

- Anthropic had established itself as a leading frontier AI lab
- Revenue had grown to several billion in ARR
- FTX estate's original ~8% stake would have been worth ~$14.6 billion at this valuation
- Competition and consolidation in AI continued to intensify

---

## Use of Funds

The Series F capital supported:

- Massive scaling of frontier model development
- Compute and infrastructure at unprecedented scale
- Global expansion and enterprise growth
- Continued safety research
