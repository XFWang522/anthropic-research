# FTX 出售 Anthropic 股份：8.84亿美元（首批）

**时间：** 2024年3月  
**金额：** 8.84亿美元  
**出售股份：** FTX 财团 Anthropic 持股约 2/3  
**主要买家：** Mubadala

---

## 概述

2024年3月，**FTX 破产财团**以 **8.84亿美元** 出售其 Anthropic 股份约 **三分之二**。**Mubadala**，阿布扎比主权财富基金，为**主要买家**。

---

## 背景

- FTX/Alameda 于 2022年4月投资约 5亿美元获得 Anthropic 约 8%（B 轮）
- FTX 于 2022年11月崩盘；股份成为破产财团资产
- Anthropic 估值大幅上升（如 2024年2月 D 轮 177亿美元）
- 财团寻求变现股份以偿还债权人

---

## 出售详情

| 指标 | 数值 |
|--------|-------|
| **出售金额** | 8.84亿美元 |
| **出售股份** | FTX 财团总持股约 2/3 |
| **主要买家** | Mubadala（阿布扎比主权财富基金） |
| **剩余股份** | 约 1/3（后续出售——见下） |

---

## Mubadala

**Mubadala Investment Company** – 阿布扎比主权财富基金，在全球科技和风险投资领域有 significant 布局。

---

## 影响

- 出售验证了 Anthropic 估值和投资者需求
- 主权财富基金对前沿 AI 日益感兴趣
- FTX 债权人受益于 Anthropic 投资的增值
- 剩余约 1/3 于 2024年6月出售（参见 [20240600-ftx-sells-remaining-shares-450m.md](20240600-ftx-sells-remaining-shares-450m.md)）

# FTX Sells Anthropic Stake: $884 Million (First Tranche)

**Date:** March 2024  
**Amount:** $884 million  
**Stake sold:** ~2/3 of FTX estate's Anthropic holdings  
**Lead buyer:** Mubadala

---

## Overview

In **March 2024**, the **FTX bankruptcy estate** sold approximately **two-thirds** of its Anthropic stake for **$884 million**. **Mubadala**, the sovereign wealth fund of Abu Dhabi, was the **lead buyer**.

---

## Background

- FTX/Alameda had invested ~$500 million for ~8% of Anthropic in April 2022 (Series B)
- FTX collapsed in November 2022; the stake became part of the bankruptcy estate
- Anthropic's valuation had risen sharply (e.g., $17.7B in Series D, February 2024)
- The estate sought to monetize the stake to repay creditors

---

## Sale Details

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Sale amount** | $884 million |
| **Stake sold** | ~2/3 of total FTX estate holdings |
| **Lead buyer** | Mubadala (Abu Dhabi sovereign wealth fund) |
| **Remaining stake** | ~1/3 (sold later – see below) |

---

## Mubadala

**Mubadala Investment Company** – Abu Dhabi's sovereign wealth fund, with significant technology and venture investments globally.

---

## Implications

- The sale validated Anthropic's valuation and investor demand
- Sovereign wealth funds were increasingly interested in frontier AI
- FTX creditors benefited from the appreciation of the Anthropic investment
- The remaining ~1/3 was sold in June 2024 (see [20240600-ftx-sells-remaining-shares-450m.md](20240600-ftx-sells-remaining-shares-450m.md))
