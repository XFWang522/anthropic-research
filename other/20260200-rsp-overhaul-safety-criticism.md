# RSP 改革与安全批评

**时间：** 2026年2月  
**主题：** 负责任扩展政策（RSP）v3.0 发布及批评

---

## 概述

2026年2月，Anthropic 发布了 **RSP v3.0**（负责任扩展政策），这是其安全与扩展框架的重大更新。批评者认为新版本**削弱了承诺**，Anthropic 已**"抛弃了核心安全承诺"**。Anthropic 高管以**竞争对手快速推进**作为变更理由。**EA Forum** 将此举描述为**"悄悄倒退"**。

---

## RSP v3.0 的变化

RSP v3.0 相较早期版本引入了重大变化：

- **前沿安全路线图** – 安全规划的新结构
- **风险报告** – 每3–6个月发布
- **区分单边与行业承诺** – Anthropic 单独承诺的事项与倡导行业标准的事项之间的区分

---

## 批评

### "抛弃核心安全承诺"

批评者认为 RSP v3.0：

- 移除或削弱了早期版本中的具体承诺
- 降低了扩展条件的严格程度
- 使安全阈值更加灵活或模糊

### EA Forum："悄悄倒退"

- **有效利他主义论坛**将此次更新定性为悄悄收回先前承诺
- 担忧商业压力正在压倒安全优先事项

### Anthropic 的回应

- **高管**表示**竞争对手正在快速推进**，过于僵化的承诺可能使 Anthropic 处于不利地位，而无法改善行业整体安全
- 将更新定位为更实用、更灵活的方法

---

## 背景

- Anthropic 曾将自己定位为安全领导者；RSP 是其关键差异化因素
- AI 行业正处于激烈竞争期（2025–2026年）
- 关于 AI 安全和扩展的监管与政策辩论持续进行

# RSP Overhaul and Safety Criticism

**Date:** February 2026  
**Topic:** Responsible Scaling Policy (RSP) v3.0 release and criticism

---

## Overview

In **February 2026**, Anthropic released **RSP v3.0** (Responsible Scaling Policy), a major update to its safety and scaling framework. Critics argued that the new version **weakened commitments** and that Anthropic had **"ditched core safety promise."** Anthropic executives cited **competitors advancing quickly** as a reason for the changes. The **EA Forum** described the move as **"quietly backpedalling."**

---

## RSP v3.0 Changes

RSP v3.0 introduced significant changes from earlier versions:

- **Frontier Safety Roadmaps** – New structure for safety planning
- **Risk Reports** – Published every 3–6 months
- **Separated unilateral from industry-wide** – Distinction between what Anthropic commits to alone vs. what it advocates for industry-wide

---

## Criticism

### "Ditched Core Safety Promise"

Critics argued that RSP v3.0:

- Removed or weakened specific commitments from earlier versions
- Reduced the stringency of scaling conditions
- Made safety thresholds more flexible or vague

### EA Forum: "Quietly Backpedalling"

- The **Effective Altruism Forum** characterized the update as quietly walking back previous commitments
- Concern that commercial pressure was overriding safety priorities

### Anthropic's Response

- **Executives** stated that **competitors were advancing quickly** and that overly rigid commitments could disadvantage Anthropic without improving industry-wide safety
- Framed the update as a more practical, adaptable approach

---

## Context

- Anthropic had positioned itself as a safety leader; RSP was a key differentiator
- The AI industry was in a period of intense competition (2025–2026)
- Regulatory and policy debates continued over AI safety and scaling
