# Anthropic A 轮：1.24亿美元

**时间：** 2021年5月  
**轮次：** A 轮  
**金额：** 1.24亿美元（$124M）

---

## 概述

Anthropic 于 2021年5月完成 **A 轮** 融资 **1.24亿美元**，紧随其正式创立。该轮由在商业成功和长期思维方面均有 strong track records 的知名科技投资者领投。

---

## 领投方

**Jaan Tallinn** – Skype 联合创始人、剑桥存在风险研究中心（CSER）创始人。Tallinn 是 AI 安全和存在风险研究的 significant 支持者。

---

## 主要投资者

| 投资者 | 背景 |
|----------|------------|
| **Jaan Tallinn** | Skype 联合创始人、CSER 创始人、AI 安全倡导者 |
| **Dustin Moskovitz** | Facebook 联合创始人、Asana CEO、Open Philanthropy |
| **Eric Schmidt** | 前 Google CEO 和董事长 |

---

## 投资者画像

投资者群体反映 Anthropic 的双重 focus：

- **商业可行性** – Schmidt 和 Moskovitz 带来扩展科技公司的经验
- **长期与安全 focus** – Tallinn 和 Moskovitz（通过 Open Philanthropy）支持 AI 安全和有效利他主义事业

---

## 资金用途

A 轮资金支持：

- 研究团队扩展
- Constitutional AI 和安全方法开发
- 早期模型开发（Claude 之前）
- 基础设施和算力

A 轮估值详情未公开披露。

# Anthropic Series A: $124 Million

**Date:** May 2021  
**Round:** Series A  
**Amount:** $124 million

---

## Overview

Anthropic closed its **Series A** funding round of **$124 million** in May 2021, shortly after its formal founding. The round was led by prominent technology investors with strong track records in both commercial success and long-term thinking.

---

## Lead Investor

**Jaan Tallinn** – Co-founder of Skype and founder of the Centre for the Study of Existential Risk (CSER) at Cambridge. Tallinn has been a significant backer of AI safety and existential risk research.

---

## Key Investors

| Investor | Background |
|----------|------------|
| **Jaan Tallinn** | Skype co-founder, CSER founder, AI safety advocate |
| **Dustin Moskovitz** | Facebook co-founder, Asana CEO, Open Philanthropy |
| **Eric Schmidt** | Former Google CEO and Chairman |

---

## Investor Profile

The investor group reflected Anthropic's dual focus:

- **Commercial viability** – Schmidt and Moskovitz brought experience scaling technology companies
- **Long-term and safety focus** – Tallinn and Moskovitz (via Open Philanthropy) have supported AI safety and effective altruism causes

---

## Use of Funds

The Series A capital supported:

- Expansion of the research team
- Development of Constitutional AI and safety methodologies
- Early model development (pre-Claude)
- Infrastructure and compute

---

## Valuation

Valuation details for the Series A round were not publicly disclosed.
