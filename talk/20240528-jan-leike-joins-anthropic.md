## Summary

Jan Leike announced on X/Twitter that he was joining Anthropic from OpenAI. He expressed excitement to "continue the superalignment mission" and described his new team's focus: scalable oversight, weak-to-strong generalization, and automated alignment research. The announcement came amid broader discussions about AI safety priorities across the industry.

---

## Key Points

### Joining Anthropic from OpenAI

- Jan Leike previously led alignment research at OpenAI
- Move to Anthropic represented a significant industry shift
- Anthropic's safety-first culture and mission alignment were factors
- High-profile hire for Anthropic's alignment capabilities

### Continuing the Superalignment Mission

- "Excited to continue the superalignment mission"
- Superalignment: aligning AI systems that may be smarter than humans
- Core challenge of AI safety—ensuring powerful systems remain aligned
- Anthropic as a place to pursue this research

### New Team Focus Areas

- **Scalable oversight**: How to supervise AI systems that may exceed human capability
- **Weak-to-strong generalization**: Using weaker models to supervise stronger ones
- **Automated alignment research**: Using AI to help with alignment research
- Building on prior work from both OpenAI and Anthropic

### Industry Context

- Alignment research talent is scarce and highly sought
- Moves between labs reflect different organizational priorities
- Anthropic's public benefit structure and safety focus attract alignment researchers
- Jan's hire strengthened Anthropic's alignment team
