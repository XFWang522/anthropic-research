## 概述

Anthropic 七位联合创始人承诺将 80% 的财富用于慈善，估计总额超过 210 亿美元。承诺聚焦于应对 AI 驱动的 inequality，反映创始人确保 AI 广泛造福人类而非仅惠及建造者或拥有者的承诺。类似「捐赠誓言」的承诺是 AI 最有价值公司之一的重要声明。

---

## 要点

### 80% 财富承诺

- 七位联合创始人均承诺捐出 80% 财富
- 在科技界不寻常——许多亿万富翁捐赠比例较小
- 反映对使命的深切承诺
- 财富主要为 Anthropic 股权——价值可能增长

### 估计超过 210 亿美元

- 按 Anthropic 估值，创始人股权可观
- 210 亿美元以上是保守估计——可能更高
- 科技史上最大慈善承诺之一
- 捐赠时间待定

### 聚焦 AI 驱动的 inequality

- AI 可能加剧 inequality——收益集中、岗位替代
- 慈善用于：教育、再培训、普及、安全网
- 确保收益广泛分配
- 长期社会影响

### 联合创始人包括

- Daniela Amodei、Dario Amodei、Tom Brown、Jack Clark、Jared Kaplan、Sam McCandlish、Chris Olah
- 创始团队完全一致
- 为公司文化定调
- 与纯营利实验室区分

### 捐赠誓言背景

- 类似巴菲特/盖茨捐赠誓言
- 承诺在有生之年或遗嘱中捐赠
- 公开承诺建立问责
- 更广泛亿万富翁慈善运动的一部分

## Summary

The seven co-founders of Anthropic pledged 80% of their wealth to philanthropy, with an estimated total of $21B+. The pledge focuses on addressing AI-driven inequality and reflects the founders' commitment to ensuring that AI benefits humanity broadly, not just those who build or own it. The Giving Pledge-style commitment was a significant statement from one of AI's most valuable companies.

---

## Key Points

### 80% Wealth Pledge

- All seven co-founders committed to giving 80% of their wealth
- Unusual in tech—many billionaires give smaller percentages
- Reflects deep commitment to mission
- Wealth largely in Anthropic equity—value could grow

### Estimated $21B+

- At Anthropic's valuation, founders' equity is substantial
- $21B+ is a conservative estimate—could be higher
- One of the largest philanthropic pledges in tech history
- Timing of giving to be determined

### Focus on AI-Driven Inequality

- AI could exacerbate inequality—concentration of gains, displacement
- Philanthropy to address: education, retraining, access, safety nets
- Ensuring benefits are broadly distributed
- Long-term societal impact

### Co-Founders Included

- Daniela Amodei, Dario Amodei, Tom Brown, Jack Clark, Jared Kaplan, Sam McCandlish, Chris Olah
- Full alignment among founding team
- Sets tone for company culture
- Differentiates Anthropic from purely profit-driven labs

### Giving Pledge Context

- Similar to Buffett/Gates Giving Pledge
- Commitment to give during lifetime or in will
- Public commitment creates accountability
- Part of broader billionaire philanthropy movement
