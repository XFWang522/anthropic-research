## 完整内容

### 80% 财富承诺

2026 年 1 月下旬，Anthropic 的七位联合创始人——Dario Amodei、Daniela Amodei、Tom Brown、Jack Clark、Jared Kaplan、Sam McCandlish 和 Christopher Olah——承诺将个人财富的 80% 捐赠给慈善事业。七位联合创始人均承诺捐出 80% 财富。在科技界不寻常——许多亿万富翁捐赠比例较小。反映对使命的深切承诺。财富主要为 Anthropic 股权——价值可能增长。

### 估计超过 210 亿美元

根据 2025 年 12 月估计，每位联合创始人约 37 亿美元，七位创始人的承诺总额超过 210 亿美元。然而，考虑到 Anthropic 不断上升的估值，这一数额可能大幅增长；公司于 2026 年 2 月完成 300 亿美元融资，投后估值 3800 亿美元，这将使个人股权价值可能达到每位创始人 70-100 亿美元或更多。科技史上最大慈善承诺之一。捐赠时间待定。

### 目的与动机

这些联合创始人都是前 OpenAI 领导人，旨在应对先进 AI 带来的社会风险，特别是极端财富集中和经济 disruption。CEO Dario Amodei 警告，AI 可能推动全球 GDP 每年 10-20% 的增长，同时取代大量白领工作并集中前所未有的财富。他表示：「要担心的是将打破社会的财富集中程度。」AI 可能加剧不平等——收益集中、岗位替代。慈善用于：教育、再培训、普及、安全网。确保收益广泛分配。长期社会影响。

### 更广泛的倡议

这一承诺伴随着 Amodei 的随笔「技术的青春期」。Anthropic 员工也承诺了价值数十亿美元的股份，公司匹配捐赠。Amodei 敦促其他富裕科技人物自愿效仿，同时支持累进税作为潜在后备。类似巴菲特/盖茨捐赠誓言。承诺在有生之年或遗嘱中捐赠。公开承诺建立问责。更广泛亿万富翁慈善运动的一部分。

### 联合创始人名单

Daniela Amodei、Dario Amodei、Tom Brown、Jack Clark、Jared Kaplan、Sam McCandlish、Chris Olah。创始团队完全一致。为公司文化定调。与纯营利实验室区分。

### 背景：AI 与就业

Amodei 最近几个月对 AI 威胁做出了引人注目的预测：它可能消除一半的入门级白领工作，软件工程师可能一年内过时。在达沃斯，Amodei 与 DeepMind 的 Demis Hassabis、Anthropic 的 Dario Amodei、Yann LeCun 讨论了 AI 的影响。

## Full Content

### 80% Wealth Pledge

In late January 2026, Anthropic's seven co-founders—Dario Amodei, Daniela Amodei, Tom Brown, Jack Clark, Jared Kaplan, Sam McCandlish, and Christopher Olah—pledged to donate 80% of their personal wealth to philanthropic efforts. All seven co-founders committed to giving 80% of their wealth. Unusual in tech—many billionaires give smaller percentages. Reflects deep commitment to mission. Wealth largely in Anthropic equity—value could grow.

### Estimated $21B+

Based on December 2025 estimates of approximately $3.7 billion per co-founder, the pledge totals over $21 billion across all seven founders. However, this amount could grow substantially given Anthropic's rising valuation; the company closed a $30 billion funding round in February 2026 at a $380 billion post-money valuation, which would increase individual stakes to potentially $7–10 billion or more per founder. One of the largest philanthropic pledges in tech history. Timing of giving to be determined.

### Purpose and Motivation

The co-founders, all former OpenAI leaders, aim to address societal risks from advanced AI, particularly extreme wealth concentration and economic disruption. CEO Dario Amodei warned that AI could drive 10–20% annual global GDP growth while displacing large swaths of white-collar jobs and concentrating unprecedented wealth. He stated: "The thing to worry about is a level of wealth concentration that will break society." AI could exacerbate inequality—concentration of gains, displacement. Philanthropy to address: education, retraining, access, safety nets. Ensuring benefits are broadly distributed. Long-term societal impact.

### Broader Initiative

The commitment was accompanied by Amodei's essay "The Adolescence of Technology." Anthropic employees have also pledged shares worth billions, with the company matching donations. Amodei urged other wealthy tech figures to follow suit voluntarily, while supporting progressive taxation as a potential fallback. Similar to Buffett/Gates Giving Pledge. Commitment to give during lifetime or in will. Public commitment creates accountability. Part of broader billionaire philanthropy movement.

### Co-Founders Included

Daniela Amodei, Dario Amodei, Tom Brown, Jack Clark, Jared Kaplan, Sam McCandlish, Chris Olah. Full alignment among founding team. Sets tone for company culture. Differentiates Anthropic from purely profit-driven labs.

### Context: AI and Jobs

Amodei has made head-turning predictions about the threat of AI in recent months: that it could eliminate half of all entry-level white-collar jobs, with software engineers just a year out from being obsolete. At Davos, Amodei discussed AI impact with DeepMind's Demis Hassabis, Anthropic's Dario Amodei, and Yann LeCun.
