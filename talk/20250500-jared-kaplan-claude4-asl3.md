## 概述

Jared Kaplan 宣布 Claude 4 因其生物能力被指定为 Anthropic 负责任扩展政策下的 ASL-3（AI 安全等级 3）。这触发了更严格的安全条款。他将 RSP 描述为在 AI 安全上创造「竞相向上」的竞赛，公司竞相实施稳健的保障措施。

---

## 要点

### Claude 4 与 ASL-3

- Claude 4 达到触发 RSP 下 ASL-3 的能力
- ASL-3 与生物能力相关——可能协助生物研究或风险
- 更高安全等级需要更严格措施
- 指定反映 Anthropic 对透明风险评估的承诺

### 生物能力

- ASL-3 模型可能协助生物研究
- 包括与合成生物学、药物发现等相关的能力
- 需要谨慎评估双重用途风险
- 更严格的访问控制和监控

### 更严格安全条款

- ASL-3 触发额外安全要求
- 可能包括：增强监控、访问限制、评估协议
- 表明 RSP 具有约束力——能力阈值有后果
- 主动而非被动方法

### RSP 创造「竞相向上」

- RSP 激励公司实施强安全措施
- 公开承诺建立问责
- 竞争对手可采用类似框架
- 行业范围安全实践改进

## Summary

Jared Kaplan announced that Claude 4 had been designated ASL-3 (AI Safety Level 3) under Anthropic's Responsible Scaling Policy due to its biological capabilities. This triggered stricter safety provisions. He framed RSP as creating a "race to the top" in AI safety, where companies compete to implement robust safeguards.

---

## Key Points

### Claude 4 and ASL-3

- Claude 4 reached capabilities that triggered ASL-3 under RSP
- ASL-3 is associated with biological capabilities—potential to assist in biological research or risks
- Higher safety level requires more stringent measures
- Designation reflects Anthropic's commitment to transparent risk assessment

### Biological Capabilities

- Models at ASL-3 can potentially assist with biological research
- Includes capabilities relevant to synthetic biology, drug discovery, etc.
- Requires careful evaluation of dual-use risks
- Stricter access controls and monitoring

### Stricter Safety Provisions

- ASL-3 triggers additional safety requirements
- May include: enhanced monitoring, access restrictions, evaluation protocols
- Demonstrates that RSP has teeth—capability thresholds have consequences
- Proactive rather than reactive approach

### RSP Creating "Race to the Top"

- RSP incentivizes companies to implement strong safety measures
- Public commitment creates accountability
- Competitors can adopt similar frameworks
- Industry-wide improvement in safety practices
