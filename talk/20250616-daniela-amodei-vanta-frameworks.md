## 概述

Daniela Amodei 在 Vanta「增长框架」活动中与 Christina Cacioppo 对话。她讨论了 Anthropic 招聘「10x AI 工程师」的方法、文化驱动招聘、预测成功的非显而易见特质、公益公司结构以及负责任扩展。对话强调扩展需要的不仅是人数——需要对的人和文化。

---

## 要点

### 招聘「10x AI 工程师」

- Anthropic 寻求卓越的 AI 人才
- 「10x」指影响力，而不仅是个人生产力
- 寻找能提升整个团队的人
- 竞争激烈的市场——使命和文化是差异化因素

### 文化驱动招聘

- 文化契合与技术技能同样重要
- 使命对齐——关心安全的人
- 协作、严谨、谦逊的价值观
- 为你想成为的公司招聘

### 非显而易见特质

- 超越简历：好奇心、学术诚实、改变想法的能力
- 对不确定性的舒适——AI 快速演进
- 沟通与协作
- 长期思维

### 公益公司

- PBC 结构吸引使命导向的候选人
- 表明利润不是唯一目标
- 支持艰难决策（如安全优先于速度）
- 与纯商业实验室区分

### 负责任扩展

- 扩展不仅是增长——是以正确方式做
- 在规模下保持文化困难
- 流程必须支持而非替代判断
- 负责任扩展包括安全和治理

## Summary

Daniela Amodei joined Christina Cacioppo for a conversation at Vanta's "Frameworks for Growth" event. She discussed Anthropic's approach to hiring "10x AI engineers," culture-driven hiring, non-obvious traits that predict success, the public benefit corporation structure, and scaling responsibly. The conversation emphasized that scaling requires more than just headcount—it requires the right people and culture.

---

## Key Points

### Hiring "10x AI Engineers"

- Anthropic seeks exceptional AI talent
- "10x" refers to impact, not just individual productivity
- Look for people who elevate the whole team
- Competitive market—mission and culture are differentiators

### Culture-Driven Hiring

- Culture fit is as important as technical skill
- Mission alignment—people who care about safety
- Values around collaboration, rigor, and humility
- Hiring for the company you want to become

### Non-Obvious Traits

- Beyond resumes: curiosity, intellectual honesty, ability to change mind
- Comfort with uncertainty—AI is rapidly evolving
- Communication and collaboration
- Long-term thinking

### Public Benefit Corporation

- PBC structure attracts mission-oriented candidates
- Signals that profit is not the only goal
- Enables difficult decisions (e.g., safety over speed)
- Differentiates from purely commercial labs

### Scaling Responsibly

- Scaling is not just growth—it's doing it right
- Maintaining culture at scale is hard
- Processes must support, not replace, judgment
- Responsible scaling includes safety and governance

---

## Notable Quotes

> "We hire for 10x impact, not just individual productivity."

> "Culture-driven hiring means mission alignment matters as much as skills."

> "Non-obvious traits: curiosity, intellectual honesty, comfort with uncertainty."

> "Scaling responsibly means maintaining our values as we grow."
