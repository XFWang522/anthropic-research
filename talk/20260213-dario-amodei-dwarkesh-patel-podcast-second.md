# 达里奥·阿莫迪第二次做客 Dwarkesh 播客

**日期：** 2026年2月13日

**平台/场合：** Dwarkesh 播客

**演讲者：** 达里奥·阿莫迪，Anthropic 首席执行官

**时长：** 2 小时 22 分钟

## 背景/语境

这是达里奥·阿莫迪第二次做客 Dwarkesh 播客，距第一次近两年半。延长的时长（2小时22分）允许进行深入的技术和战略讨论。语境已发生重大变化：Anthropic 已发展到巨大规模，AI 能力已大幅提升，政策辩论已加剧。播客形式比典型媒体露面更能进行坦诚讨论，阿莫迪的第二次露面因其在时间线、竞争和监管方面的直接性而著称。

## 要点

- **「我们接近指数阶段的尾声」：** 阿莫迪表示我们正在接近 AI 进展指数阶段的尾声——暗示可预测的扩展改进时期可能即将结束，对未来意味着什么

- **AGI「仅需数年」：** 给出了具体时间线：AGI「仅需数年」。这是他关于近期 AGI 最直接的陈述之一

- **任务特定强化学习：** 讨论了任务特定强化学习及其在推进能力方面的作用。探讨了 RL 方法如何超越通用预训练而演变

- **扩展假设：** 重新探讨扩展定律和扩展假设——更多算力、数据和模型规模可预测地提升性能。讨论了是否正在看到这种方法的极限

- **收入预测：** 分享了更新的收入预测和商业指标，反映 Anthropic 的增长轨迹

- **算力承诺：** 讨论了算力承诺和基础设施投资——保持前沿所需的资本

- **监管：** 探讨了监管格局，包括自他早期露面以来发生了什么变化以及他对未来的预期

- **中国：** 讨论了中国 AI 发展、出口管制和 AI 竞争的地缘政治维度

- **出口管制：** 探讨了美国对 AI 技术的出口管制以及国家安全与商业考量之间的平衡

## 重要引言

- 「我们接近指数阶段的尾声。」

- 「AGI 仅需数年。」

- 关于扩展：「扩展假设一直成立——但我们可能正在接近其极限。」

- 关于监管：「格局已经改变。我们处于不同阶段。」

## 总结

阿莫迪第二次做客 Dwarkesh 因其观点的若干更新而引人注目。「接近指数阶段的尾声」暗示与他早期 framing 的转变——我们可能正在从可预测扩展阶段过渡到某种新事物。「AGI 仅需数年」是具体的时间线陈述。任务特定 RL 和扩展极限的讨论表明了技术上的细微差别。收入和算力讨论提供了商业语境。监管和中国/出口管制讨论反映了演变后的政策环境。2小时22分的时长允许了使 Dwarkesh 播客成为 AI 话语关键场所的深度。第二次露面展示了阿莫迪的观点在近两年半的快速变化中如何演变——或 refined。

# Dario Amodei's Second Appearance on the Dwarkesh Podcast

**Date:** February 13, 2026

**Platform/Venue:** Dwarkesh Podcast

**Speaker:** Dario Amodei, CEO of Anthropic

**Duration:** 2 hours 22 minutes

## Background/Context

This was Dario Amodei's second appearance on the Dwarkesh Podcast, nearly two and a half years after his first. The extended format (2h22m) allowed for deep technical and strategic discussion. The context had changed significantly: Anthropic had grown to massive scale, AI capabilities had advanced substantially, and policy debates had intensified. The podcast format enables more candid discussion than typical media appearances, and Amodei's second appearance was noted for its directness on timelines, competition, and regulation.

## Key Points

- **"We Are Near the End of the Exponential":** Amodei stated that we are approaching the end of the exponential phase of AI progress—suggesting that the period of predictable scaling improvements may be concluding, with implications for what comes next

- **AGI "Just a Few Years Away":** Gave a concrete timeline: AGI is "just a few years away." This was among his most direct statements on near-term AGI

- **Task-Specific RL:** Discussed task-specific reinforcement learning and its role in advancing capabilities. Addressed how RL approaches are evolving beyond general pretraining

- **Scaling Hypothesis:** Revisited scaling laws and the scaling hypothesis—the idea that more compute, data, and model size predictably improve performance. Discussed whether we're seeing the limits of this approach

- **Revenue Projections:** Shared updated revenue projections and business metrics, reflecting Anthropic's growth trajectory

- **Compute Commitments:** Discussed compute commitments and infrastructure investments—the capital required to remain at the frontier

- **Regulation:** Addressed the regulatory landscape, including what has changed since his earlier appearances and what he expects going forward

- **China:** Discussed China's AI development, export controls, and the geopolitical dimension of AI competition

- **Export Controls:** Addressed U.S. export controls on AI technology and the balance between national security and commercial considerations

## Notable Quotes

- "We are near the end of the exponential."

- "AGI is just a few years away."

- On scaling: "The scaling hypothesis has held—but we may be approaching its limits."

- On regulation: "The landscape has changed. We're in a different phase."

## Summary

Amodei's second Dwarkesh appearance was notable for several updates to his views. "Near the end of the exponential" suggested a shift from his earlier framing—we may be transitioning from a phase of predictable scaling to something new. "AGI just a few years away" was a concrete timeline statement. The discussion of task-specific RL and scaling limits indicated technical nuance. The revenue and compute discussions provided business context. The regulation and China/export control discussions reflected the evolved policy environment. The 2h22m format allowed for the depth that has made the Dwarkesh Podcast a key venue for AI discourse. The second appearance showed how Amodei's views had evolved—or been refined—over nearly two and a half years of rapid change.
