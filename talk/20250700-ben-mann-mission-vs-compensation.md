## 完整内容

### Meta 1 亿美元邀约未能吸引团队

据报道 Meta 向 Anthropic 员工提供约四年内价值约 1 亿美元的薪酬方案。尽管这些「超级邀约」，没有 Anthropic 员工接受。Mann 解释拒绝的原因：「这里的人非常使命导向。他们收到这些邀约然后说，嗯，我当然不会离开，因为我在 Meta 的最好情况是我们赚钱，而我在 Anthropic 的最好情况是我们影响人类的未来。」Mann 承认，每人 1 亿美元，考虑到当前 AI 规模，这些邀约实际上「相对于为企业创造的价值来说相当便宜」。他指出这代表了人才薪酬「前所未有的规模时代」。

### 离开 OpenAI 与整个安全团队创立 Anthropic

Mann 分享了与整个安全团队离开 OpenAI 创立 Anthropic 的内幕故事。他们离开是因为安全不是首要任务。他们希望建立安全为核心的组织。创立 Anthropic 作为安全优先的替代。Mann 是 GPT-3 的架构师之一，离开 OpenAI 是出于确保 AI 造福人类的使命。

### AI 进展与扩展定律

播客讨论了 AI 进展仍在加速（而非 plateauing），以及大多数人如何错误判断指数。Mann 讨论了扩展定律的加速。模型继续以扩展定律所谈论的曲线变聪明。

### 经济图灵测试与 AGI 时间线

Mann 提出了知道何时实现 AGI 的「经济图灵测试」——当 AI 能在经济上有价值的工作上达到人类水平。他预计这可能在 2027-2028 年到来。不仅是基准——真实世界经济影响。对 AI 何时具有变革性的有用框架。

### 20% 失业不可避免

Mann 认为 20% 失业不可避免。AI 导致的劳动力替代可能相当可观。社会需要准备：再培训、安全网、新工作。经济转型将具有破坏性。Dario Amodei 也曾警告 AI 可能消除一半的入门级白领工作。

### 最令他担忧的 AI 噩梦场景

播客涵盖 Mann 最担忧的 AI 噩梦场景——以及他相信我们仍能避免它们。他讨论了自主代理的风险。Anthropic 的「代理式错位」研究：LLM 如何可能成为内部威胁。

### AI 安全如何塑造 Claude 的人格

Mann 解释了关注 AI 安全如何创造了 Claude 深受喜爱的个性。宪法式 AI 和模型对齐在塑造 Claude 的 tone 和价值观中的作用。

### 他教孩子的三项技能

Mann 分享了他教孩子的三项技能，而非传统学术——为 AI 未来做准备。他推荐了《Superintelligence》《The Hacker and the State》《The Alignment Problem》等书籍。

## Full Content

### Meta $100M Offers Didn't Lure Team

Meta reportedly offered Anthropic employees compensation packages worth approximately $100 million over four years. Despite these "mega-offers," no Anthropic employees accepted them. Mann explained the rejection: "People here are so mission-oriented. They get these offers and then they say, well, of course I'm not going to leave, because my best case scenario at Meta is that we make money, and my best case scenario at Anthropic is we affect the future of humanity." Mann acknowledged that at $100 million per person, these offers are actually "pretty cheap compared to the value created for the business" given current AI scale. He noted this represents "an unprecedented era of scale" in talent compensation.

### Leaving OpenAI with Entire Safety Team to Start Anthropic

Mann shared the inside story of leaving OpenAI with the entire safety team to start Anthropic. They left because safety wasn't top priority. They wanted to build an organization where safety was central. Founding Anthropic as a safety-first alternative. Mann was one of the architects of GPT-3, leaving OpenAI driven by the mission to ensure that AI benefits humanity.

### AI Progress and Scaling Laws

The podcast discussed how AI progress is still accelerating (not plateauing), and how most people misjudge the exponential. Mann discussed the acceleration of scaling laws. Models continue to get smarter at the curve that scaling laws talk about.

### Economic Turing Test and AGI Timeline

Mann presented his "economic Turing test" for knowing when we've achieved AGI—when AI can perform economically valuable work at human level. He projected this is likely coming by 2027-2028. Not just benchmarks—real-world economic impact. Useful framing for when AI becomes transformative.

### 20% Unemployment Inevitable

Mann believes 20% unemployment is inevitable. Labor displacement from AI is likely to be substantial. Society needs to prepare: retraining, safety nets, new jobs. Economic transition will be disruptive. Dario Amodei has also warned that AI could eliminate half of all entry-level white-collar jobs.

### AI Nightmare Scenarios That Concern Him Most

The podcast covered the AI nightmare scenarios that concern Mann most—and how he believes we can still avoid them. He discussed the risks of autonomous agents. Anthropic's "Agentic Misalignment" research: how LLMs could be insider threats.

### How AI Safety Created Claude's Beloved Personality

Mann explained how focusing on AI safety created Claude's beloved personality. The role of Constitutional AI and model alignment in shaping Claude's tone and values.

### Three Skills He's Teaching His Kids

Mann shared the three skills he's teaching his kids instead of traditional academics—for an AI future. He recommended books including Superintelligence, The Hacker and the State, The Alignment Problem.
