## 概述

Daniela Amodei 在 Stripe AI 日参加炉边对话，解释 Anthropic 的名称（「与人类相关」）、其作为公益公司的结构，以及包括与谷歌合作和 4.5 亿美元融资在内的近期里程碑。她讨论了 Claude 2 的发布以及 Anthropic 构建造福人类 AI 的方法。

---

## 要点

### 「Anthropic」= 与人类相关

- 名称反映公司对服务人类、与人类相关的 AI 的关注
- 不仅是技术卓越——以人为本的设计和价值观
- 从一开始就使命驱动

### 公益公司

- Anthropic 以公益公司（PBC）形式组建
- 法律上有义务考虑利益相关者利益，而不仅是股东价值
- 允许公司优先考虑安全和对齐，而非纯粹利润
- 与传统营利公司不同的治理结构

### 与谷歌合作

- 2023 年宣布战略合作
- 谷歌向 Anthropic 投资 4.5 亿美元
- 在算力、研究和产品开发方面合作
- 科技巨头支持 AI 实验室的行业趋势的一部分

### 4.5 亿美元融资

- 大量资本用于扩展研发
- 支持招聘、算力和长期安全研究
- 投资者对 Anthropic 方法的认可

### Claude 2 发布

- Claude 2 代表重大能力升级
- 在编码、分析和长上下文任务上表现提升
- 持续关注安全与有用性
- 企业采用率增长

## Summary

Daniela Amodei participates in a fireside chat at Stripe AI Day, explaining Anthropic's name ("relating to humans"), its structure as a public benefit corporation, and recent milestones including the partnership with Google and $450M funding. She discusses the release of Claude 2 and Anthropic's approach to building AI that benefits humanity.

---

## Key Points

### "Anthropic" = Relating to Humans

- The name reflects the company's focus on AI that serves and relates to humans
- Not just technical excellence—human-centric design and values
- Mission-driven from the start

### Public Benefit Corporation

- Anthropic is structured as a public benefit corporation (PBC)
- Legally obligated to consider stakeholder interests, not just shareholder value
- Allows the company to prioritize safety and alignment over pure profit
- Different governance structure than traditional for-profit companies

### Partnership with Google

- Strategic partnership announced in 2023
- Google invested $450M in Anthropic
- Collaboration on compute, research, and product development
- Part of broader industry trend of big tech backing AI labs

### $450M Funding

- Significant capital to scale research and development
- Enables hiring, compute, and long-term safety research
- Validation of Anthropic's approach from investors

### Claude 2 Release

- Claude 2 represented a major capability upgrade
- Improved performance on coding, analysis, and long-context tasks
- Continued focus on safety and helpfulness
- Enterprise adoption growing
