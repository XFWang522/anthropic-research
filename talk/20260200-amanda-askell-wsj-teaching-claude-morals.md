## 完整内容

### Anthropic 的哲学家

Amanda Askell，Anthropic 的驻场哲学家，创建了一份约 3 万字的 Claude 指令手册，已非正式地被称为 AI 模型的「灵魂」。这份全面的文档跨越超过 100 页的详细提示， governs Claude 如何推理对错、处理道德困境、参与敏感话题以及回应 emotionally charged 对话。Askell，一位来自苏格兰农村的牛津培养的哲学家，在 2021 年 Anthropic 创立时加入，此前曾在 OpenAI 工作。作为人格对齐团队负责人，她通过美德伦理学和 AI 对齐方面的工作塑造 Claude 的品格和宪法。

### 教 Claude 道德

对齐不仅是技术——涉及道德和哲学推理。Askell 的角色：灌输价值观、原则和判断。迭代的优化和评估过程。人类价值观复杂——编码困难。给 Claude 的部分指导非常详尽，不仅是简短指令——详细场景、原则、示例，反映人类价值观的复杂性。

### 超过 100 页的提示词

宪法及相关材料体量可观。融合多种传统的原则——联合国宣言、苹果服务条款等。活文档——随时间更新。

### 工作被比作「养育孩子」

2026 年 2 月华尔街日报报道突出了 Anthropic 如何独特地信任 Askell 塑造模型的道德推理和人格，将她的角色比作「养育孩子」——训练是非感、建立情商。类比：塑造 Claude 的价值观如同养育孩子。需要耐心、一致性、纠正、强化。结果无法完全控制。道德发展是持续的。

### 3 万字提示词作为 Claude 的「灵魂」

Askell 撰写了定义 Claude 核心价值观的 3 万字提示词。被描述为 Claude 的「灵魂」——其性格的本质。融合多种传统的原则（联合国宣言、苹果服务条款等）。活文档——随时间更新。

### AI 将「不可避免地形成自我感」

Askell 相信 AI 模型将「不可避免地形成自我感」，人类现在如何与它们互动和对待它们将很重要。她还承诺将 10% 的终身收入和一半股权捐赠给慈善机构，并在 2024 年被列入 TIME100 AI 名单。

### 主流关注

WSJ 报道将对齐工作带给普通受众。通常不可见——在幕后进行。Askell 作为人格对齐的公众面孔。揭开「教 AI」的含义。

## Full Content

### Anthropic's Philosopher

Amanda Askell, Anthropic's resident philosopher, created a roughly 30,000-word instruction manual for Claude that has become known informally as the AI model's "soul." This comprehensive document, which spans over 100 pages of detailed prompts, governs how Claude reasons about right and wrong, handles moral dilemmas, engages with sensitive topics, and responds to emotionally charged conversations. Askell, an Oxford-educated philosopher from rural Scotland, joined Anthropic at its founding in 2021 after previously working at OpenAI. As head of the personality alignment team, she shapes Claude's character and constitution through her work in virtue ethics and AI alignment.

### Teaching Claude Morals

Alignment is not just technical—it involves moral and philosophical reasoning. Askell's role: instilling values, principles, and judgment. Iterative process of refinement and evaluation. Human values are complex—encoding them is hard. Some of the guidance given to Claude is extensive—not just short instructions but detailed scenarios, principles, examples, reflecting the complexity of human values.

### Prompts Exceeding 100 Pages

The Constitution and related materials are substantial. Combines principles from many traditions—UN Declaration, Apple ToS, etc. Living document—updated over time.

### Work Compared to "Raising a Child"

A Wall Street Journal profile from February 2026 highlighted how Anthropic uniquely entrusts Askell with shaping the model's moral reasoning and personality, comparing her role to "raising a child"—training a sense of right and wrong and building emotional intelligence. Analogy: shaping Claude's values is like raising a child. Requires patience, consistency, correction, reinforcement. Outcomes are not fully controllable. Moral development is ongoing.

### 30,000-Word Prompt as Claude's "Soul"

Askell authored a 30,000-word prompt that defines Claude's core values. Described as Claude's "soul"—the essence of its character. Combines principles from many traditions (UN Declaration, Apple ToS, etc.). Living document—updated over time.

### AI Will "Inevitably Form Senses of Self"

Askell believes that AI models will "inevitably form senses of self" and that how humans interact with and treat them now will matter. She has also committed to donating 10 percent of her lifetime income and half her equity to charity, and was named to the TIME100 AI list in 2024.

### Mainstream Attention

WSJ profile brought alignment work to general audience. Often invisible—happens behind the scenes. Askell as public face of personality alignment. Demystifies what "teaching AI" means.
