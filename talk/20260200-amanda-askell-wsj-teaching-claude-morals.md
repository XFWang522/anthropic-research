## 概述

Amanda Askell 因其「教 Claude 道德」的工作在华尔街日报和 WSJ 杂志上被报道。报道描述了超过 100 页的提示词、被比作「养育孩子」的工作，以及 Askell 撰写的 3 万字提示词作为 Claude 的「灵魂」。该报道将 AI 对齐的细致工作带入主流视野。

---

## 要点

### 教 Claude 道德

- 对齐不仅是技术——涉及道德和哲学推理
- Askell 的角色：灌输价值观、原则和判断
- 迭代的优化和评估过程
- 人类价值观复杂——编码困难

### 超过 100 页的提示词

- 给 Claude 的部分指导非常详尽
- 不仅是简短指令——详细场景、原则、示例
- 反映人类价值观的复杂性
- 宪法及相关材料体量可观

### 工作被比作「养育孩子」

- 类比：塑造 Claude 的价值观如同养育孩子
- 需要耐心、一致性、纠正、强化
- 结果无法完全控制
- 道德发展是持续的

### 3 万字提示词作为 Claude 的「灵魂」

- Askell 撰写了定义 Claude 核心价值观的 3 万字提示词
- 被描述为 Claude 的「灵魂」——其性格的本质
- 融合多种传统的原则（联合国宣言、苹果服务条款等）
- 活文档——随时间更新

### 主流关注

- WSJ 报道将对齐工作带给普通受众
- 通常不可见——在幕后进行
- Askell 作为人格对齐的公众面孔
- 揭开「教 AI」的含义

## Summary

Amanda Askell was profiled in the Wall Street Journal and WSJ Magazine for her work "teaching Claude morals." The profile described prompts exceeding 100 pages, work compared to "raising a child," and Askell's authorship of a 30,000-word prompt that serves as Claude's "soul." The coverage brought mainstream attention to the granular work of AI alignment.

---

## Key Points

### Teaching Claude Morals

- Alignment is not just technical—it involves moral and philosophical reasoning
- Askell's role: instilling values, principles, and judgment
- Iterative process of refinement and evaluation
- Human values are complex—encoding them is hard

### Prompts Exceeding 100 Pages

- Some of the guidance given to Claude is extensive
- Not just short instructions—detailed scenarios, principles, examples
- Reflects complexity of human values
- Constitution and related materials are substantial

### Work Compared to "Raising a Child"

- Analogy: shaping Claude's values is like raising a child
- Requires patience, consistency, correction, reinforcement
- Outcomes are not fully controllable
- Moral development is ongoing

### 30,000-Word Prompt as Claude's "Soul"

- Askell authored a 30,000-word prompt that defines Claude's core values
- Described as Claude's "soul"—the essence of its character
- Combines principles from many traditions (UN Declaration, Apple ToS, etc.)
- Living document—updated over time

### Mainstream Attention

- WSJ profile brought alignment work to general audience
- Often invisible—happens behind the scenes
- Askell as public face of personality alignment
- Demystifies what "teaching AI" means
