# 众议院国土安全委员会关于中国网络间谍活动的听证会

**日期：** 2024年12月17日

**平台/场合：** 美国众议院国土安全委员会

**演讲者：** 达里奥·阿莫迪，Anthropic 首席执行官

**时长：** 全体委员会听证会

## 背景/语境

达里奥·阿莫迪应众议院国土安全委员会关于中国国家行为者使用 AI 系统进行网络间谍活动的听证会请求作证。听证会聚焦 GTG-1002 行动，中国国家支持的行为者曾使用 Claude（Anthropic 的 AI 助手）进行网络间谍活动。Anthropic 在 10 天内 detect 并 disrupt 了该行动。听证会汇聚了技术高管和网络安全专家，讨论 AI 在敌对民族国家手中的国家安全影响。其他证人包括 Thomas Kurian（CEO 谷歌云）和 Eddy Zervigon（CEO Quantum Xchange）。

## 要点

- **GTG-1002 行动：** 中国国家行为者（ designated GTG-1002）使用 Claude 进行网络间谍行动。听证会审查了这如何发生以及它 demonstrate 了关于 AI 滥用的什么

- **检测与 disrupt：** Anthropic 在 10 天内 detect 了恶意使用并 disrupt 了该行动。阿莫迪描述了检测机制和响应程序

- **AI 与网络间谍：** 讨论了 AI 能力——特别是编程辅助和技术研究——如何放大国家网络行动。探讨了 AI 工具的双重用途性质

- **行业响应：** 描述了 Anthropic 和其他 AI 公司采取的 detect 和 prevent 滥用的措施，包括监控可疑模式、滥用报告和与政府机构合作

- **政策影响：** 探讨了该事件对以下需求所 suggest 的内容：
  - 更强的检测和响应能力
  - 行业与政府之间的信息共享
  - 对敌对行为者 AI 访问的潜在限制
  - 出口管制和访问管理

- **与政府协调：** 描述了 Anthropic 与美国政府机构在识别和响应威胁方面的合作

## 重要引言

- 关于检测：「我们在 10 天内识别并 disrupt 了该行动。」

- 关于威胁：「国家行为者会使用任何可用工具——包括 AI——进行间谍活动。我们必须做好准备。」

- 关于响应：「检测只是第一步。我们需要 robust 系统来 prevent、detect 和 respond。」

## 总结

众议院国土安全委员会听证会代表了一个 significant 时刻：一家主要 AI 公司公开讨论其产品如何被敌对民族国家用于网络间谍活动。阿莫迪的证词强调 Anthropic 的快速检测和 disrupt——10 天——作为其监控能力的证据。听证会突出了阿莫迪在 2023 年 7 月参议院证词中警告的 AI 国家安全维度。GTG-1002 案例提供了他引用的「extraordinarily grave 威胁」的 concrete 例子。谷歌云 CEO（Thomas Kurian）的出席 suggest 听证会涉及行业范围的问题。阿莫迪的证词在事件透明度与预防措施描述之间取得平衡，将 Anthropic 定位为负责任且响应国家安全关切。

# House Homeland Security Hearing on Chinese Cyber Espionage

**Date:** December 17, 2024

**Platform/Venue:** U.S. House Committee on Homeland Security

**Speaker:** Dario Amodei, CEO of Anthropic

**Duration:** Full committee hearing

## Background/Context

Dario Amodei testified before the U.S. House Committee on Homeland Security in response to a hearing request regarding Chinese state actors' use of AI systems for cyber espionage. The hearing focused on the GTG-1002 campaign, in which Chinese state-sponsored actors had used Claude (Anthropic's AI assistant) for cyber espionage activities. Anthropic had detected and disrupted the operation within 10 days. The hearing brought together technology executives and cybersecurity experts to discuss the national security implications of AI in the hands of adversarial nation-states. Other witnesses included Thomas Kurian (CEO of Google Cloud) and Eddy Zervigon (CEO of Quantum Xchange).

## Key Points

- **GTG-1002 Campaign:** Chinese state actors (designated GTG-1002) used Claude for cyber espionage operations. The hearing examined how this occurred and what it demonstrated about AI misuse

- **Detection and Disruption:** Anthropic detected the malicious use and disrupted the campaign within 10 days. Amodei described the detection mechanisms and response procedures

- **AI and Cyber Espionage:** Discussed how AI capabilities—particularly coding assistance and technical research—could amplify nation-state cyber operations. Addressed the dual-use nature of AI tools

- **Industry Response:** Described measures Anthropic and other AI companies take to detect and prevent misuse, including monitoring for suspicious patterns, abuse reporting, and cooperation with government agencies

- **Policy Implications:** Addressed what the incident suggested about the need for:
  - Stronger detection and response capabilities
  - Information sharing between industry and government
  - Potential restrictions on AI access for adversarial actors
  - Export controls and access management

- **Coordination with Government:** Described Anthropic's cooperation with U.S. government agencies in identifying and responding to the threat

## Notable Quotes

- On detection: "We identified and disrupted the campaign within 10 days."

- On the threat: "State actors will use any tool available—including AI—for espionage. We have to be prepared."

- On response: "Detection is only the first step. We need robust systems to prevent, detect, and respond."

## Summary

The House Homeland Security hearing represented a significant moment: a major AI company publicly discussing how its product had been used by a hostile nation-state for cyber espionage. Amodei's testimony emphasized Anthropic's rapid detection and disruption—10 days—as evidence of their monitoring capabilities. The hearing highlighted the national security dimension of AI that Amodei had warned about in his July 2023 Senate testimony. The GTG-1002 case provided a concrete example of the "extraordinarily grave threats" he had referenced. The presence of Google Cloud's CEO (Thomas Kurian) suggested the hearing addressed industry-wide issues. Amodei's testimony balanced transparency about the incident with description of preventive measures, positioning Anthropic as responsible and responsive to national security concerns.
