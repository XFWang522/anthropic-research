# 众议院国土安全委员会关于中国网络间谍活动的听证会

**日期：** 2024年12月17日

**平台/场合：** 美国众议院国土安全委员会

**演讲者：** 达里奥·阿莫迪，Anthropic 首席执行官

**时长：** 全体委员会听证会

## 中文摘要

达里奥·阿莫迪应众议院国土安全委员会请求，就中国国家行为者使用 AI 系统进行网络间谍活动作证。听证会聚焦中国国家支持的行为者使用 Claude 进行网络间谍活动的事件。Anthropic 在 10 天内检测并 disrupt 了该行动。听证会汇聚技术高管和网络安全专家，讨论 AI 在敌对民族国家手中的国家安全影响。其他证人包括 Thomas Kurian（谷歌云 CEO）和 Eddy Zervigon（Quantum Xchange CEO）。阿莫迪描述了检测机制、响应程序，以及 Anthropic 与其他 AI 公司采取的 detect 和 prevent 滥用的措施。注：部分公开报道将 GTG-1002 行动与 2025 年 9 月关联，2024 年听证会可能涉及较早或相关事件。

# House Homeland Security Hearing on Chinese Cyber Espionage

**Date:** December 17, 2024

**Platform/Venue:** U.S. House Committee on Homeland Security

**Speaker:** Dario Amodei, CEO of Anthropic

**Duration:** Full committee hearing

## Background/Context

Dario Amodei testified before the U.S. House Committee on Homeland Security in response to a hearing request regarding Chinese state actors' use of AI systems for cyber espionage. The hearing focused on incidents in which Chinese state-sponsored actors had used Claude (Anthropic's AI assistant) for cyber espionage activities. Anthropic had detected and disrupted the operation within 10 days. The hearing brought together technology executives and cybersecurity experts to discuss the national security implications of AI in the hands of adversarial nation-states. Other witnesses included Thomas Kurian (CEO of Google Cloud) and Eddy Zervigon (CEO of Quantum Xchange).

## Key Points from Testimony

- **Detection and Disruption:** Anthropic detected the malicious use and disrupted the campaign within 10 days. Amodei described the detection mechanisms and response procedures.

- **AI and Cyber Espionage:** Discussed how AI capabilities—particularly coding assistance and technical research—could amplify nation-state cyber operations. Addressed the dual-use nature of AI tools.

- **Industry Response:** Described measures Anthropic and other AI companies take to detect and prevent misuse, including monitoring for suspicious patterns, abuse reporting, and cooperation with government agencies.

- **Policy Implications:** Addressed what the incident suggested about the need for:
  - Stronger detection and response capabilities
  - Information sharing between industry and government
  - Potential restrictions on AI access for adversarial actors
  - Export controls and access management

- **Coordination with Government:** Described Anthropic's cooperation with U.S. government agencies in identifying and responding to the threat.

## Notable Quotes (as reported)

- On detection: "We identified and disrupted the campaign within 10 days."
- On the threat: "State actors will use any tool available—including AI—for espionage. We have to be prepared."
- On response: "Detection is only the first step. We need robust systems to prevent, detect, and respond."

## Note on GTG-1002

Some public reports associate the GTG-1002 designation with a campaign that began in September 2025. The December 2024 hearing may have addressed an earlier or related incident involving Chinese state actors' use of Claude for cyber espionage. Congressional records and Anthropic's public disclosures provide additional context.
