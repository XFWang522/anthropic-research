## 概述

Daniela Amodei 回顾了组建 Anthropic 创始团队的经历、AI 创业公司与传统科技公司的独特动态，以及与兄弟 Dario Amodei 共同创业的体验。她讨论了早期招聘理念、Claude 2 的发布，以及定义 Anthropic 历程的 AI 快速演进。

---

## 要点

### 组建创始团队

- 七位联合创始人，技能互补
- 技术（机器学习、可解释性、系统）与非技术（政策、运营）专长相结合
- 对安全和使命的共同承诺
- 从一开始就明确分工

### AI 创业公司的差异

- AI 创业公司遵循不同的时间线——能力变化迅速
- 需要灵活性和快速调整能力
- 人才稀缺且竞争激烈
- 监管和政策环境不断演变

### 与兄弟姐妹共同创业

- Daniela 和 Dario Amodei 共同创立 Anthropic
- 独特动态：深厚信任、共同历史、潜在冲突
- 明确角色有助于——Dario 专注研究/技术，Daniela 专注运营/领导
- 家庭纽带可强化使命一致性

### 早期招聘

- 优先考虑使命一致性和文化契合
- 寻找深切关心安全的人
- 技术卓越必要但不足
- 建立能随公司扩展的团队

### Claude 2 与 AI 快速演进

- Claude 2 代表重大进展
- AI 演进比许多人预测的更快
- 需要持续适应
- 在保持安全的同时领先于能力曲线

## Summary

Daniela Amodei reflects on building Anthropic's founding team, the unique dynamics of AI startups compared to traditional tech companies, and the experience of starting a company with her sibling (Dario Amodei). She discusses early hiring philosophy, the release of Claude 2, and the rapid evolution of AI that has defined Anthropic's journey.

---

## Key Points

### Building the Founding Team

- Seven co-founders with complementary skills
- Mix of technical (ML, interpretability, systems) and non-technical (policy, operations) expertise
- Shared commitment to safety and mission
- Division of responsibilities from the start

### AI Startup Differences

- AI startups operate on different timelines—capabilities change rapidly
- Requires flexibility and ability to pivot
- Talent is scarce and highly competitive
- Regulatory and policy landscape is evolving

### Starting a Company with a Sibling

- Daniela and Dario Amodei co-founded Anthropic
- Unique dynamics: deep trust, shared history, potential for conflict
- Clear roles help—Dario focused on research/technical, Daniela on operations/leadership
- Family connection can strengthen mission alignment

### Early Hiring

- Prioritized mission alignment and cultural fit
- Looked for people who cared deeply about safety
- Technical excellence necessary but not sufficient
- Built a team that could scale with the company

### Claude 2 and Rapid AI Evolution

- Claude 2 represented significant progress
- AI evolution has been faster than many predicted
- Constant adaptation required
- Staying ahead of capability curve while maintaining safety
