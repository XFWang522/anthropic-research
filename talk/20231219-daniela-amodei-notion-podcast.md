## 完整内容

### 从运营者到创始人

Daniela Amodei 在 Notion「First Block」播客第 5 集中与 Akshay Kothari 对话，回顾从运营者到创始人的转型。这期约 40 分钟的节目涵盖了她与兄弟 Dario Amodei 共同创业、联合创始人关系与团队动态、Anthropic 早期招聘、Claude 2 的开发，以及 AI 技术的快速演进。

### 与兄弟姐妹共同创业

Amodei 讨论了与兄弟 Dario 共同创立 Anthropic 的独特体验。她表示，从 Stripe 获得灵感，亲眼目睹 John 和 Patrick 兄弟共同创办公司。她很幸运与 Dario 拥有极其互补的技能组合，合作一直非常愉快。他们从小就希望一起做点什么，曾在 OpenAI 共事，但自童年起就梦想能一起构建某样东西。对她来说，每天与他共事是一种巨大的荣幸。他们从左右脑不同角度共同解决问题的能力非常独特。她见过许多组织或企业中的伟大合作，但与认识一生的人一起做这件事，有一种非常特别的感觉。

### Anthropic 的独特方法

Amodei 强调，Anthropic 在头一年半大量投入研究，直到 2023 年才组建上市团队。她指出，从 Claude 1 到 Claude 2 的跃升，比 GPT-2 到 Claude 1 之间的差距更大，凸显 AI 演进的速度之快。

### 平衡多重角色

Amodei 描述了她广泛角色的平衡——管理技术研究、商业战略和公司文化建设的多个团队。她将这种体验比作「飞机起飞时还在建造它」。

### 组建创始团队

七位联合创始人拥有互补技能，结合技术（机器学习、可解释性、系统）与非技术（政策、运营）专长。对安全和使命的共同承诺从一开始就明确。分工从起步就清晰——Dario 专注研究/技术，Daniela 专注运营/领导。

### AI 创业公司的差异

AI 创业公司遵循不同的时间线——能力变化迅速。需要灵活性和快速调整能力。人才稀缺且竞争激烈。监管和政策环境不断演变。

### 早期招聘

优先考虑使命一致性和文化契合。寻找深切关心安全的人。技术卓越必要但不足。建立能随公司扩展的团队。

### Claude 2 与 AI 快速演进

Claude 2 代表重大进展。AI 演进比许多人预测的更快。需要持续适应。在保持安全的同时领先于能力曲线。

### 社区反馈循环

Amodei 讨论了在构建 AI 产品时社区反馈循环的重要性，以及如何将用户洞察纳入迭代开发。

## Full Content

### Transition from Operator to Founder

Daniela Amodei joined Notion's "First Block" podcast, Episode 5, for a conversation with Akshay Kothari, reflecting on her transition from operator to founder. The approximately 40-minute episode covered her experience starting a company with her brother Dario Amodei, co-founder relationships and team dynamics, early hiring at Anthropic, development of Claude 2, and the rapid evolution of AI technology.

### Starting a Company with a Sibling

Amodei discussed the unique experience of co-founding Anthropic with her brother Dario. She noted that she had great inspiration from Stripe because she saw John and Patrick co-found a company together up close. She's really fortunate that Dario and she have extremely complimentary skill sets. It's actually just been really fun. They've always wanted to work on something together. They worked together at OpenAI. But since they were little kids, they've always wanted to be able to build something together. For her, it's actually a huge privilege to be able to get to work with him every day. She thinks their ability to tackle problems together from the left brain, right brain perspective is very unique. And she has seen many great partnerships like that in organizations or businesses before. But there's something very special about someone that you've known your entire life, being able to do it with them.

### Anthropic's Unique Approach

Amodei emphasized that Anthropic invested heavily in research for the first year and a half before developing a go-to-market team in 2023. She noted that the jump from Claude 1 to Claude 2 represented greater improvement than the gap between GPT-2 and Claude 1, highlighting how rapidly AI has been evolving.

### Balancing Broad Role

Amodei described balancing her broad role overseeing managers across technical research, commercial strategy, and company culture building—describing the experience as "building the airplane while it's taking off."

### Building the Founding Team

Seven co-founders with complementary skills. Mix of technical (ML, interpretability, systems) and non-technical (policy, operations) expertise. Shared commitment to safety and mission. Division of responsibilities from the start—Dario focused on research/technical, Daniela on operations/leadership.

### AI Startup Differences

AI startups operate on different timelines—capabilities change rapidly. Requires flexibility and ability to pivot. Talent is scarce and highly competitive. Regulatory and policy landscape is evolving.

### Early Hiring

Prioritized mission alignment and cultural fit. Looked for people who cared deeply about safety. Technical excellence necessary but not sufficient. Built a team that could scale with the company.

### Claude 2 and Rapid AI Evolution

Claude 2 represented significant progress. AI evolution has been faster than many predicted. Constant adaptation required. Staying ahead of capability curve while maintaining safety.

### Community Feedback Loops

Amodei discussed the importance of community feedback loops in building AI products and how user insights inform iterative development.
