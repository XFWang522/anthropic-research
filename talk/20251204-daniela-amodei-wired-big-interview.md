## 概述

Daniela Amodei 接受 WIRED 深度访谈，与 Steven Levy 对话，主张「市场将奖励安全的 AI」，并不同意特朗普政府认为监管会扼杀 AI 发展的观点。她讨论了宪法式 AI、劳动力替代，以及 Anthropic 相信安全与商业成功是一致的。访谈将 Anthropic 定位为既使命驱动又商业可行。

---

## 要点

### 市场将奖励安全的 AI

- 企业和消费者将偏好可信的 AI
- 安全是竞争优势，而非成本中心
- 偷工减料的公司将面临声誉和法律风险
- 长期而言，安全的 AI 胜出

### 不同意特朗普政府关于监管的观点

- 特朗普政府曾暗示监管会扼杀 AI
- Amodei 不同意——明智的监管可以促进创新
- 监管可创造公平竞争环境和消费者信心
- 行业与政府可以合作

### 宪法式 AI

- Anthropic 的对齐方法——模型由原则指导
- 可扩展、一致的价值观应用
- 减少有害输出并建立信任
- Anthropic 相信能在安全上竞争的原因之一

### 劳动力替代

- AI 将替代部分工作岗位——已承认的现实
- 社会需要准备：再培训、安全网、新产业
- Anthropic 参与政策问题
- 不仅是技术问题——是社会问题

### 商业与使命

- Anthropic 既使命驱动又商业成功
- 收入增长验证了方法
- 安全与商业可以一致
- 公益公司结构支持两者

## Summary

Daniela Amodei sat for the WIRED Big Interview with Steven Levy, arguing that "the market will reward safe AI" and disagreeing with the Trump administration's view that regulation would cripple AI development. She discussed Constitutional AI, labor displacement, and Anthropic's belief that safety and commercial success are aligned. The interview positioned Anthropic as both mission-driven and commercially viable.

---

## Key Points

### The Market Will Reward Safe AI

- Enterprises and consumers will prefer AI they can trust
- Safety is a competitive advantage, not a cost center
- Companies that cut corners will face reputational and legal risk
- Long-term, safe AI wins

### Disagrees with Trump Admin on Regulation

- Trump administration had suggested regulation would cripple AI
- Amodei disagrees—smart regulation can foster innovation
- Regulation can create level playing field and consumer confidence
- Industry and government can work together

### Constitutional AI

- Anthropic's approach to alignment—models guided by principles
- Scalable, consistent application of values
- Reduces harmful outputs and builds trust
- Part of why Anthropic believes it can compete on safety

### Labor Displacement

- AI will displace some jobs—acknowledged reality
- Society needs to prepare: retraining, safety nets, new industries
- Anthropic engages on policy questions
- Not just a technical problem—societal

### Commercial vs. Mission

- Anthropic is both mission-driven and commercially successful
- Revenue growth validates the approach
- Safety and business can align
- Public benefit corporation structure supports both
