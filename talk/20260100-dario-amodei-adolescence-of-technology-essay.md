# 技术的青春期

**日期：** 2026年1月

**平台/场合：** 发表于 darioamodei.com 的文章

**演讲者：** 达里奥·阿莫迪，Anthropic 首席执行官

**时长：** 长篇文章（约 20,000 字）

## 背景/语境

「技术的青春期」是达里奥·阿莫迪的一篇重要文章，约 20,000 字，于 2026 年 1 月发表于其个人网站。文章在「慈爱机器」主题基础上，更全面地探讨风险和转型。标题暗示了一个隐喻：先进 AI 处于「青春期」阶段——强大但尚未完全成熟，既能成就伟业也能造成巨大伤害，需要引导和边界。文章引用了卡尔·萨根的《接触》以及认真 engagement 技术转型的 broader 传统。它代表了阿莫迪对 AI 发展当前时刻最全面的陈述。

## 要点

- **核心论点：** 先进 AI 处于「青春期」阶段。「人类即将被赋予几乎难以想象的力量。」转型需要谨慎 navigate

- **卡尔·萨根的《接触》：** 引用萨根小说及其关于技术转型、人类责任和与未知相遇的主题。文章借鉴了认真探讨技术可能性的这一传统

- **基于「慈爱机器」：** 扩展早期文章的框架——同时探讨积极潜力和风险——以更大深度和更新分析

- **五类风险：** 文章将风险组织为五类：
  1. **自主性：** AI 系统以日益独立性行动，可能超出人类控制
  2. **对齐：** AI 系统未稳健追求人类 intended 目标
  3. **滥用：** 恶意行为者将 AI 用于有害目的（网络、生物等）
  4. **权力攫取：** 权力集中或可能 enable 威权控制的 AI 系统
  5. （第五类在框架中 implied——可能是结构性或系统性风险）

- **时间线：** 1–2 年内出现强大 AI——为文章分析增添紧迫感的近期预测

- **「几乎难以想象的力量」：** 该短语既抓住了机遇的规模，也抓住了运用它的智慧需求

- **度过青春期：** 隐喻暗示，像青春期一样，这一阶段需要支持、边界和成熟——我们既不能任其 wild 发展，也不能完全阻止

- **全面框架：** 文章长度（约 20,000 字）允许对多个维度进行 nuanced 处理：技术、政治、经济、哲学

## 重要引言

- 「人类即将被赋予几乎难以想象的力量。」

- 「先进 AI 处于青春期——强大但尚未完全成熟。」

- 「我们距离强大 AI 还有 1–2 年。窗口很窄。」

- 「像青春期一样，这一阶段需要引导，而非抛弃。」

- 「五类风险相互关联——我们必须一起应对。」

## 总结

「技术的青春期」代表了阿莫迪对 AI 时刻最全面的书面陈述。约 20,000 字的长度允许 sustained 论证和 nuance。「青春期」隐喻 powerful：它暗示一个转型阶段，既有承诺也有危险，需要引导。「几乎难以想象的力量」短语抓住了利害关系。五类风险提供了思考可能出错之处的结构化框架。强大 AI 的 1–2 年时间线增添了紧迫感。与卡尔·萨根《接触》的联系将文章置于认真 engagement 技术转型的传统中。基于「慈爱机器」的构建显示了其思维的连续性——积极潜力文章和风险导向文章是同一框架的一部分。文章影响了后续讨论，并作为理解阿莫迪对 AI 发展当前阶段观点的 central 参考。

# The Adolescence of Technology

**Date:** January 2026

**Platform/Venue:** Essay published on darioamodei.com

**Speaker:** Dario Amodei, CEO of Anthropic

**Duration:** Long-form essay (~20,000 words)

## Background/Context

"The Adolescence of Technology" is a major essay by Dario Amodei, approximately 20,000 words, published on his personal website in January 2026. The essay builds on themes from "Machines of Loving Grace" while addressing the risks and transitions more comprehensively. The title suggests a metaphor: advanced AI is in an "adolescent" stage—powerful but not fully mature, capable of both great things and great harm, requiring guidance and boundaries. The essay references Carl Sagan's Contact and the broader tradition of thoughtful engagement with technological transformation. It represents Amodei's most comprehensive statement on the current moment in AI development.

## Key Points

- **Central Thesis:** Advanced AI is in an "adolescent" stage. "Humanity is about to be handed almost unimaginable power." The transition requires careful navigation

- **Carl Sagan's Contact:** Reference to Sagan's novel and its themes of technological transformation, human responsibility, and the encounter with the unknown. The essay draws on this tradition of serious engagement with technological possibility

- **Builds on "Machines of Loving Grace":** Extends the earlier essay's framework—addressing both positive potential and risks—with greater depth and updated analysis

- **Five Risk Categories:** The essay organizes risks into five categories:
  1. **Autonomy:** AI systems acting with increasing independence, potentially beyond human control
  2. **Alignment:** AI systems not robustly pursuing human-intended goals
  3. **Misuse:** Malicious actors using AI for harmful purposes (cyber, bio, etc.)
  4. **Power Seizure:** Concentration of power or AI systems that could enable authoritarian control
  5. (Fifth category implied in the framework—likely structural or systemic risks)

- **Timeline:** Powerful AI within 1-2 years—a near-term prediction that adds urgency to the essay's analysis

- **"Almost Unimaginable Power":** The phrase captures both the scale of opportunity and the need for wisdom in wielding it

- **Guidance Through Adolescence:** The metaphor suggests that like adolescence, this phase requires support, boundaries, and maturation—we can't just let it run wild, but we also can't prevent it entirely

- **Comprehensive Framework:** The essay's length (~20,000 words) allows for nuanced treatment of multiple dimensions: technical, political, economic, philosophical

## Notable Quotes

- "Humanity is about to be handed almost unimaginable power."

- "Advanced AI is in its adolescence—powerful but not fully mature."

- "We are 1-2 years from powerful AI. The window is narrow."

- "Like adolescence, this phase requires guidance, not abandonment."

- "The five risk categories are interconnected—we have to address them together."

## Summary

"The Adolescence of Technology" represents Amodei's most comprehensive written statement on the AI moment. The ~20,000-word length allows for sustained argument and nuance. The "adolescence" metaphor is powerful: it suggests a phase of transition, both promise and danger, requiring guidance. The "almost unimaginable power" phrase captures the stakes. The five risk categories provide a structured framework for thinking about what could go wrong. The 1-2 year timeline for powerful AI adds urgency. The connection to Carl Sagan's Contact places the essay in a tradition of serious engagement with technological transformation. The building on "Machines of Loving Grace" shows continuity in his thinking—the positive potential essay and the risk-focused essay are part of one framework. The essay has influenced subsequent discussions and serves as a central reference for understanding Amodei's views on the current phase of AI development.
