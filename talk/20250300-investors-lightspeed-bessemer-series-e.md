## 完整内容

### 融资概要

Anthropic 在 2025 年 3 月完成 35 亿美元 E 轮融资，投后估值 615 亿美元。Lightspeed Venture Partners 作为最大投资者领投本轮。其他参与者包括 Bessemer Venture Partners、Cisco Investments、D1 Capital Partners、Fidelity Management & Research Company、General Catalyst、Jane Street、Menlo Ventures 和 Salesforce Ventures。Anthropic 成立于 2021 年，截至当时已累计融资 182 亿美元。

### Lightspeed 的论述

Lightspeed 在 2023 年初首次接触 Anthropic，当时公司员工不足 100 人，没有公开产品，处于 pre-revenue。两年内，Anthropic 发布了行业定义模型，与行业巨头建立了重大合作伙伴关系，并以惊人速度扩展。真正的技术拐点很少。过去几十年，我们见证了软件、互联网、云和移动从根本上改变了公司运营和人们生活的方式。但这些转变大多是平台转变。它们实现了新体验，但不是 fundamentally 新的技术能力。AI——作为软件的智能——是一个 fundamentally 不同的范式。我们正在见证以前根本不存在的全新能力的创造，它们正在一个接一个地改变领域。语言模型已开始 disrupt 内容创作、编码、分析、教育、客户支持、知识和数据库管理、决策支持、创意协作以及更多人类活动领域——现在都作为服务提供。我们看不到创新步伐放缓的证据。事实上，我们认为它可能正在加速。

### 企业 AI 拐点

Bessemer 强调，2023 年 AI 采用的初始浪潮专注于消费者应用和概念验证。但 2025 年，企业正在从实验转向生产部署。这种转变需要的不仅仅是原始模型性能——需要稳健的基础设施、可靠扩展和企业级安全。Anthropic 展示了这种企业优先方法，将最先进的能力与企业安全有效部署 AI 所需的功能相结合。

### 安全优势正在扩大

Bessemer 指出，最近的事态发展只会强化他们的信念：AI 安全不仅是伦理 imperative，还是关键的竞争优势。当其他人以最小保障 rush 上市时，Anthropic 的宪法式 AI 方法和全面安全框架对无法承受不安全 AI 部署的声誉或运营风险的企业变得越来越有价值。Anthropic 的负责任扩展政策建立了行业黄金标准，其他竞争对手难以匹配。所有这些活动都得到 Anthropic 对机制可解释性和对齐研究的持续承诺的支持。

### Claude 3.7 与 Claude Code

融资公告紧随 Claude 3.7 Sonnet（首款 generally available 混合推理模型）和 Claude Code（研究预览中的代理式编码工具）的发布。Claude Code 无缝集成到开发者工作流中，在终端内运行，理解代码库，并通过自然语言命令加速开发。

### 资金用途

Anthropic 计划将资本用于推进下一代 AI 系统、扩大算力容量、深化机制可解释性和对齐研究，以及加速在亚洲和欧洲的国际扩张。

### 商业表现

截至 2024 年底，公司年化收入 run rate 约 10 亿美元，2025 年初增长 30%。然而，Anthropic 预计 2025 年将 burn 约 30 亿美元以开发其 AI 系统。

## Full Content

### Funding Summary

Anthropic closed a $3.5 billion Series E funding round in March 2025, reaching a $61.5 billion post-money valuation. Lightspeed Venture Partners led the round as the largest investor. Other participants included Bessemer Venture Partners, Cisco Investments, D1 Capital Partners, Fidelity Management & Research Company, General Catalyst, Jane Street, Menlo Ventures, and Salesforce Ventures. Anthropic was founded in 2021 and had raised a total of $18.2 billion to date.

### Lightspeed's Thesis

Lightspeed first engaged with Anthropic in early 2023, when the company had fewer than 100 employees, no public product, and was pre-revenue. In the two years since, Anthropic has launched industry-defining models, forged major partnerships with industry giants, and scaled at a striking rate. True tipping points in technology are rare. Over the past few decades, we've seen software, internet, cloud, and mobile fundamentally change how companies operate and how people live their lives. But while these shifts were transformative, they were mostly platform shifts. AI—intelligence as software—is a fundamentally different paradigm. We are witnessing the creation of novel capabilities that simply did not exist before. We see no evidence that this pace of innovation is slowing. In fact, we think it may be accelerating.

### Enterprise AI Inflection Point

Bessemer emphasized that in 2023, the initial wave of AI adoption focused on consumer applications and proof-of-concepts. But in 2025, enterprises are moving from experimentation to production deployment. This shift demands more than just raw model performance—it requires robust infrastructure, reliable scaling, and enterprise-grade security. Anthropic demonstrates this enterprise-first approach, combining state-of-the-art capabilities with the features businesses need to deploy AI safely and effectively.

### Safety Advantage Widening

Bessemer noted that recent developments have only reinforced their belief that AI safety isn't just an ethical imperative—it's a crucial competitive advantage. While others rush to market with minimal safeguards, Anthropic's constitutional AI approach and comprehensive safety framework are becoming increasingly valuable to enterprises that can't afford the reputational or operational risks of unsafe AI deployment. Anthropic's Responsible Scaling Policy has established an industry gold standard other competitors struggle to match. All of these activities are underscored by Anthropic's continued commitment to research on mechanistic interpretability and alignment.

### Claude 3.7 and Claude Code

The funding announcement followed the launch of Claude 3.7 Sonnet (the industry's first generally available hybrid reasoning model) and Claude Code (an agentic coding tool in research preview). Claude Code seamlessly integrates into developers' workflows, operates within the terminal, understands codebases, and accelerates development with natural language commands.

### Use of Funds

Anthropic plans to use the capital to advance next-generation AI systems, expand compute capacity, deepen research in mechanistic interpretability and alignment, and accelerate international expansion in Asia and Europe.

### Business Performance

The company's annual revenue run rate was approximately $1 billion as of late 2024, with 30% growth in early 2025. However, Anthropic expects to burn approximately $3 billion in 2025 as it develops its AI systems.
