# Dario Amodei on The Times Tech Podcast

**Date:** December 20, 2024

**Platform/Venue:** The Times Tech Podcast (The Times, UK)

**Speaker:** Dario Amodei, CEO of Anthropic

**Duration:** 44 minutes

## Full Content (from coverage)

### Vision of the Future

Amodei described "powerful AI" as bringing a "golden future" that could move people "literally moved to tears by"—an emotionally charged vision of positive transformation.

### Why He Left OpenAI

He discussed his departure from OpenAI to found Anthropic, including the desire to build an organization with safety as a structural priority rather than one consideration among many. He said: "I wanted to build something where safety wasn't negotiable."

### AI Accelerating Progress

He described how AI is accelerating progress across multiple domains:
- **Biology:** Drug discovery, understanding of biological systems, potential to address disease and aging
- **Health:** Personalized medicine, diagnostics, treatment optimization
- **Neuroscience:** Understanding the brain, mental health treatments
- **Economic Development:** Leapfrogging development in poorer regions, reducing inequality
- **Governance:** Improving decision-making, reducing conflict, enhancing collective intelligence

### Balance of Optimism and Caution

He maintained his characteristic balance—expressing genuine excitement about positive potential while acknowledging risks and the need for careful development.

### UK and European Context

He addressed AI development and regulation in the UK and European context, including the relationship between US labs and international policy.

## Key Quotes

- "Powerful AI will bring a golden future—one that could move people to tears."
- "The acceleration we're seeing across biology, health, neuroscience, economic development, governance—it's real."
- "I wanted to build something where safety wasn't negotiable."
