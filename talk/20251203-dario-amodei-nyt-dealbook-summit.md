# AI 时代：纽约时报 DealBook 峰会

**日期：** 2025年12月3日

**平台/场合：** 纽约时报 DealBook 峰会，主持人 Andrew Ross Sorkin

**演讲者：** 达里奥·阿莫迪，Anthropic 首席执行官

**时长：** 峰会环节

## 背景/语境

达里奥·阿莫迪出席纽约时报 DealBook 峰会，这是由 Andrew Ross Sorkin 主办的年度商业领袖、投资者和政策制定者聚会。2025 年峰会的主题包括「AI 时代」，阿莫迪为特邀演讲者。DealBook 峰会以其高层次的商业和政策讨论而闻名，高管们常以坦诚评论制造新闻。阿莫迪出席时，Anthropic 已获得 1830 亿美元估值，成为全球最有价值的私营公司之一。

## 要点

- **AI 泡沫担忧：** 探讨了 AI 估值是否代表泡沫的问题。阿莫迪承认担忧，同时论证增长背后的实质

- **对「YOLO」方式的批评：** 批评了 AI 开发的「YOLO」（你只活一次）方式——即不顾充分安全考虑而鲁莽快速推进的想法。将 Anthropic 定位为更审慎的方式

- **估值与投资：** 1830 亿美元估值。讨论了 500 亿美元数据中心支出——反映 AI 基础设施的巨大资本需求

- **财务预测：**
  - 收入 3 年增长 10 倍
  - 2025 年预计收入 80–100 亿美元
  - 2028 年实现盈亏平衡的路径
  - 这些预测表明尽管大规模支出，仍有盈利路径

- **企业聚焦：** 强调 Anthropic 对企业客户而非消费者的聚焦——解释为出于安全考虑的有意选择。企业用例比广泛消费者部署有更多结构、监督和问责

- **资本密集度：** 500 亿美元数据中心承诺说明了 AI 的资本密集度——保持竞争力所需的大规模算力投资

- **负责任增长：** 在负责任发展的语境下框定增长和支出——同时投资能力和安全

## 重要引言

- 关于 YOLO：「我们负担不起 YOLO 方式。风险太高。」

- 关于估值：「数字很大——但我们构建的也很大。」

- 关于企业聚焦：「我们聚焦企业部分是因为更安全——更多结构、更多监督。」

- 关于盈亏平衡：「我们看到了 2028 年实现盈亏平衡的路径。」

## 总结

DealBook 峰会露面将阿莫迪置于商业和投资语境中——探讨 AI 发展的财务维度。他对「YOLO」方式的批评将 Anthropic 与任何鲁莽速度的 perception 区分开来。财务预测（2025 年 80–100 亿美元收入，2028 年盈亏平衡）为投资者和市场提供了具体数字。500 亿美元数据中心承诺说明了所需资本的规模。企业聚焦的解释将商业策略与安全联系起来——说明 Anthropic 的客户选择部分由安全考虑驱动，而不仅仅是商业机会。露面在「AI 时代」主题与阿莫迪 characteristic 的责任强调之间取得平衡，表明即使在最高级别商业峰会上，他也保持了安全导向的信息传递。

# The Age of A.I. at the NYT DealBook Summit

**Date:** December 3, 2025

**Platform/Venue:** NYT DealBook Summit with Andrew Ross Sorkin

**Speaker:** Dario Amodei, CEO of Anthropic

**Duration:** Summit session

## Background/Context

Dario Amodei appeared at the New York Times DealBook Summit, an annual gathering of business leaders, investors, and policymakers hosted by Andrew Ross Sorkin. The 2025 summit's theme included "The Age of A.I." with Amodei as a featured speaker. The DealBook Summit is known for its high-level business and policy discussions, with executives often making news with candid comments. The appearance came as Anthropic had achieved a $183 billion valuation and was among the most valuable private companies in the world.

## Key Points

- **AI Bubble Concerns:** Addressed the question of whether AI valuations represent a bubble. Amodei acknowledged concerns while arguing for the substance behind the growth

- **Criticism of "YOLO" Approach:** Criticized a "YOLO" (you only live once) approach to AI development—the idea of moving recklessly fast without adequate safety consideration. Positioned Anthropic's approach as more measured

- **Valuation and Investment:** $183 billion valuation. Discussed $50 billion spending on data centers—reflecting the enormous capital requirements of AI infrastructure

- **Financial Projections:**
  - Revenue has grown 10x over 3 years
  - $8-10 billion projected revenue for 2025
  - Path to break-even by 2028
  - These projections suggested a path to profitability despite massive spending

- **Enterprise Focus:** Emphasized Anthropic's focus on enterprise customers over consumer applications—explaining this as a deliberate choice for safety. Enterprise use cases have more structure, oversight, and accountability than broad consumer deployment

- **Capital Intensity:** The $50 billion data center commitment illustrated the capital intensity of AI—the need for massive compute investment to remain competitive

- **Responsible Growth:** Framed the growth and spending in the context of responsible development—investing in both capability and safety

## Notable Quotes

- On YOLO: "We can't afford a YOLO approach. The stakes are too high."

- On valuation: "The numbers are big—but so is what we're building."

- On enterprise focus: "We focus on enterprise in part because it's safer—more structure, more oversight."

- On break-even: "We see a path to break-even by 2028."

## Summary

The DealBook Summit appearance positioned Amodei in the business and investment context—addressing the financial dimensions of AI development. His criticism of the "YOLO" approach differentiated Anthropic from any perception of reckless speed. The financial projections ($8-10B revenue 2025, break-even by 2028) provided concrete numbers for investors and the market. The $50 billion data center commitment illustrated the scale of capital required. The enterprise focus explanation connected business strategy to safety—suggesting that Anthropic's customer choice is partly driven by safety considerations, not just commercial opportunity. The appearance balanced the "Age of A.I." theme with Amodei's characteristic emphasis on responsibility, showing that even at a high-level business summit, he maintained his safety-focused messaging.
