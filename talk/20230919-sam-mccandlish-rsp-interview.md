## 概述

Sam McCandlish 讨论了 Anthropic 的负责任扩展政策（RSP），该政策借鉴了实验室使用的生物安全等级。他承认"在划定边界时总存在一定程度的任意性"，但认为该政策针对灾难性风险，并为安全扩展 AI 提供了结构化框架。采访解释了 RSP 如何围绕 AI 开发建立问责和透明度。

---

## 要点

### RSP 与生物安全等级

- Anthropic 的负责任扩展政策借鉴生物安全等级（BSL-1 至 BL-4）
- 不同 AI 能力等级需要不同安全措施
- 随着模型能力增强，要求逐步提高
- 提供清晰的分层决策框架

### 边界的任意性

- "在划定边界时总存在一定程度的任意性"
- 承认安全等级并非完全客观
- 边界基于风险评估但涉及判断
- 对不确定性的透明是方法的一部分

### 政策针对灾难性风险

- RSP 关注可能造成大规模严重伤害的风险
- 不仅是渐进式安全改进——结构性风险降低
- 在特定能力阈值可能暂停或限制
- 激励与长期安全一致

### 实施

- RSP 是公开承诺——Anthropic 发布其政策
- 对利益相关者建立问责
- 竞争对手可采用类似框架
- 更广泛的行业负责任扩展运动的一部分

## Summary

Sam McCandlish discusses Anthropic's Responsible Scaling Policy (RSP), which draws inspiration from biosafety levels used in laboratories. He acknowledges that "there is always some level of arbitrariness in drawing boundaries" when defining safety tiers, but argues that the policy targets catastrophic risks and provides a structured framework for scaling AI safely. The interview explains how RSP creates accountability and transparency around AI development.

---

## Key Points

### RSP and Biosafety Levels

- Anthropic's Responsible Scaling Policy is modeled on biosafety levels (BSL-1 through BL-4)
- Different AI capability levels require different safety measures
- As models become more capable, requirements escalate
- Provides a clear, tiered framework for decision-making

### Arbitrariness in Boundaries

- "Always some level of arbitrariness in drawing boundaries"
- Acknowledgment that safety levels are not perfectly objective
- Boundaries are informed by risk assessment but involve judgment
- Transparency about uncertainty is part of the approach

### Policy Targets Catastrophic Risks

- RSP focuses on risks that could cause severe harm at scale
- Not just incremental safety improvements—structural risk reduction
- Pauses or restrictions possible at certain capability thresholds
- Aligns incentives with long-term safety

### Implementation

- RSP is a public commitment—Anthropic publishes its policy
- Creates accountability to stakeholders
- Competitors can adopt similar frameworks
- Part of broader industry movement toward responsible scaling
