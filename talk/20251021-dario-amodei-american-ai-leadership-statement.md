# AI 应成为人类进步的力量，而非灾难

**日期：** 2025年10月21日

**平台/场合：** anthropic.com 博客文章

**演讲者：** 达里奥·阿莫迪，Anthropic 首席执行官（通过 Anthropic）

**时长：** 博客文章/声明

## 背景/语境

Anthropic 于 2025 年 10 月在其博客上发布题为「AI 应成为人类进步的力量，而非灾难」的声明。声明探讨了美国 AI 领导力、政策立场以及 Anthropic 的增长和承诺。声明发布于 AI 监管、出口管制和 AI 公司在国家安全中的角色政策辩论加剧之际。声明将 Anthropic 定位为支持某些联邦政策、反对其他政策，并强调了其在行业中对华销售方面的独特立场。

## 要点

- **核心信息：** 「AI 应成为人类进步的力量，而非灾难」——将 AI 框定为既有积极也有消极影响，目标是引导走向积极结果

- **增长指标：** Anthropic 被描述为历史上增长最快的软件公司：
  - 仅 9 个月内收入从 10 亿美元增长到 70 亿美元年化运行率
  - 展示商业成功和 AI 能力的市场需求

- **国防部协议：** 宣布与国防部达成 2 亿美元协议，反映 Anthropic 与政府和国家安全应用的 engagement

- **政策立场：**
  - **支持：** 统一联邦 AI 标准——单一、连贯的联邦框架而非零散州监管
  - **支持：** 对中华人民共和国（PRC）公司的限制——限制中国 AI 公司访问美国技术和市场
  - **支持：** 特朗普 AI 行动计划——政府 AI 政策方法的具体要素
  - **反对：** 10 年州监管暂停——与他的纽约时报 op-ed 一致，反对 blunt 暂停提案

- **独特限制：** Anthropic 被描述为唯一限制向 PRC 控制公司销售的前沿 AI 公司——行业中的 significant 差异化因素和对国家安全的 concrete 承诺

- **美国领导力：** 声明将对这些政策的支持定位为美国 AI 领导力的 essential——在管理风险的同时保持美国优势

## 重要引言

- 「AI 应成为人类进步的力量，而非灾难。」

- 「我们是历史上增长最快的软件公司——仅 9 个月内从 10 亿美元到 70 亿美元年化运行率。」

- 「我们是唯一限制向 PRC 控制公司销售的前沿 AI 公司。」

- 「我们支持统一联邦标准、对 PRC 公司的限制和特朗普 AI 行动计划。我们反对 10 年暂停。」

## 总结

美国 AI 领导力声明代表了 Anthropic 在政策上最全面的公开定位。它结合了若干要素：(1) 增长叙事——9 个月内从 10 亿美元到 70 亿美元——建立商业 credibility；(2) 政府 engagement——2 亿美元国防部协议——显示愿意与国防合作；(3) 具体政策 endorsements 和 oppositions；(4) 独特差异化——前沿实验室中唯一 PRC 限制。声明对特朗普 AI 行动计划的支持反映了与政府方法的 engagement。对 10 年暂停的反对与阿莫迪 earlier 纽约时报 op-ed 一致。PRC 限制——作为唯一实施的前沿公司——是可能具有商业影响的 significant 承诺。总体而言，声明将 Anthropic 定位为支持美国 AI 领导力的负责任行业领袖，通过技术卓越和政策 engagement 实现。

# AI Should Be a Force for Human Progress, Not Peril

**Date:** October 21, 2025

**Platform/Venue:** Blog post on anthropic.com

**Speaker:** Dario Amodei, CEO of Anthropic (via Anthropic)

**Duration:** Blog post / statement

## Background/Context

Anthropic published a statement on its blog titled "AI Should Be a Force for Human Progress, Not Peril" in October 2025. The statement addressed American AI leadership, policy positions, and Anthropic's growth and commitments. It came during a period of heightened policy debate about AI regulation, export controls, and the role of AI companies in national security. The statement positioned Anthropic as supporting certain federal policies while opposing others, and highlighted its unique position in the industry regarding sales to China.

## Key Points

- **Core Message:** "AI should be a force for human progress, not peril"—framing AI as having potential for both positive and negative impact, with the goal of steering toward positive outcomes

- **Growth Metrics:** Anthropic described as the fastest-growing software company in history:
  - Revenue growth from $1 billion to $7 billion run rate in just 9 months
  - Demonstrating both commercial success and market demand for AI capabilities

- **DoD Agreement:** Announced a $200 million agreement with the Department of Defense, reflecting Anthropic's engagement with government and national security applications

- **Policy Positions:**
  - **Supports:** Unified federal AI standard—a single, coherent federal framework rather than piecemeal state regulation
  - **Supports:** Restrictions on PRC (People's Republic of China) firms—limiting Chinese AI companies' access to U.S. technology and markets
  - **Supports:** Trump AI Action Plan—specific elements of the administration's AI policy approach
  - **Opposes:** 10-year state regulation moratorium—consistent with his NYT op-ed, opposing blunt pause proposals

- **Unique Restriction:** Anthropic described as the only frontier AI company restricting sales to PRC-controlled companies—a significant differentiator in the industry and a concrete commitment to national security

- **American Leadership:** The statement positioned support for these policies as essential for American AI leadership—maintaining U.S. advantage while managing risks

## Notable Quotes

- "AI should be a force for human progress, not peril."

- "We are the fastest-growing software company in history—$1 billion to $7 billion run rate in just 9 months."

- "We are the only frontier AI company restricting sales to PRC-controlled companies."

- "We support a unified federal standard, restrictions on PRC firms, and the Trump AI Action Plan. We oppose a 10-year moratorium."

## Summary

The American AI Leadership statement represented Anthropic's most comprehensive public positioning on policy. It combined several elements: (1) growth narrative—$1B to $7B in 9 months—establishing commercial credibility; (2) government engagement—$200M DoD agreement—showing willingness to work with defense; (3) specific policy endorsements and oppositions; (4) unique differentiation—only PRC restriction among frontier labs. The statement's support for the Trump AI Action Plan reflected engagement with the administration's approach. The opposition to the 10-year moratorium was consistent with Amodei's earlier NYT op-ed. The PRC restriction—being the only frontier company to implement it—was a significant commitment that could have commercial implications. Overall, the statement positioned Anthropic as a responsible industry leader that supports American AI leadership through both technical excellence and policy engagement.
