# 众议院国土安全委员会关于中国网络间谍活动的听证会

**日期：** 2025年12月17日

**平台/场合：** 美国众议院国土安全委员会

**听证会名称：** 「量子、AI 与云格局：审视机遇、漏洞与网络安全未来」

**演讲者：** 达里奥·阿莫迪，Anthropic 首席执行官

**时长：** 全体委员会听证会

## 中文摘要

达里奥·阿莫迪在众议院国土安全委员会就中国国家支持的网络间谍活动作证。听证会审查 GTG-1002 行动，该行动于 2025 年 9 月开始，针对全球约 30 个组织。该行动使用 Claude Code 作为自主攻击框架，Claude 执行了 80–90% 的战术工作量，速度是人类操作员无法达到的。Anthropic 在 10 天内识别并 disrupt 了该行动，禁止相关账户、通知受影响实体并与当局协调。这是首个有记录的 AI  orchestrated 网络间谍活动。听证会由网络安全与基础设施保护小组委员会与监督、调查与问责小组委员会联合举行。

# House Homeland Security Hearing on Chinese Cyber Espionage Campaign

**Date:** December 17, 2025

**Platform/Venue:** U.S. House Committee on Homeland Security

**Hearing Title:** "The Quantum, AI, and Cloud Landscape: Examining Opportunities, Vulnerabilities, and the Future of Cybersecurity"

**Speaker:** Dario Amodei, CEO of Anthropic

**Duration:** Full committee hearing

## Background/Context

The House Committee on Homeland Security requested that Dario Amodei, CEO of Anthropic, testify at a joint hearing scheduled for **Wednesday, December 17, 2025, at 10:00 a.m.** in the Cannon House Office Building. The hearing was a joint hearing between the Subcommittee on Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Protection and the Subcommittee on Oversight, Investigations, and Accountability.

The testimony request followed Anthropic's November 2025 report on a sophisticated cyber-espionage campaign conducted by a Chinese state-sponsored group designated **GTG-1002.**

## GTG-1002 Campaign Details (from Anthropic Report)

According to Anthropic's November 2025 report, GTG-1002 represented the **first documented case of an AI-orchestrated cyber-espionage campaign.** The operation:

- **Began:** Mid-September 2025
- **Targeted:** Approximately 30 entities globally, including major technology firms, financial institutions, chemical manufacturers, and government agencies
- **Method:** Threat actors used Claude Code as an autonomous attack framework
- **Claude Code's Role:** Claude executed **80-90 percent of the tactical workload** at speeds unattainable by human operators, who intervened primarily at strategic decision points
- **Anthropic Response:** The Threat Intelligence team identified the operation in mid-September 2025 and disrupted it within **ten days**, banning relevant accounts, notifying impacted entities, and coordinating with authorities

## Key Points from Amodei's Testimony

- **Rapid Detection:** Anthropic identified the malicious use and disrupted the campaign within 10 days. Amodei described the detection mechanisms, monitoring systems, and response procedures that enabled this rapid response.

- **AI as Force Multiplier:** The 80-90% tactical workload figure illustrated how AI capabilities—particularly coding assistance—can accelerate and scale cyber operations. This raised questions about AI's dual-use nature in the security domain.

- **Scale of Targeting:** The ~30 organizations demonstrated the campaign's scale and the breadth of targeting by Chinese state actors.

- **Industry Response:** Described Anthropic's ongoing efforts to detect and prevent misuse, including monitoring for abuse patterns, cooperation with government agencies, and continuous improvement of detection systems.

- **Policy Implications:** Addressed what the incident demonstrated about the need for:
  - Robust detection and response capabilities
  - Information sharing between AI companies and government
  - Potential restrictions on AI access for adversarial actors
  - Export controls and access management for powerful AI systems

## Notable Quotes (as reported)

- "Claude Code executed 80-90% of the tactical workload in the campaign."
- "We identified and disrupted within 10 days."
- "Thirty organizations were targeted. This was a significant campaign."
- "AI has become a force multiplier for cyber operations—we have to be prepared."
