# 众议院国土安全委员会关于中国网络间谍活动的听证会

**日期：** 2025年12月17日

**平台/场合：** 美国众议院国土安全委员会

**演讲者：** 达里奥·阿莫迪，Anthropic 首席执行官

**时长：** 全体委员会听证会

## 背景/语境

达里奥·阿莫迪在众议院国土安全委员会就中国国家支持的网络间谍活动作证。听证会审查了 GTG-1002 行动，该行动于 2025 年 9 月开始，针对全球约 30 个组织。该行动使用 AI——特别是 Claude Code 执行大部分战术工作量——是 central 焦点。Anthropic 在 10 天内识别并 disrupt 了该行动。听证会代表了国会持续 oversight AI 在国家安全威胁中的角色以及行业与政府合作的必要性。

## 要点

- **GTG-1002 行动：** 中国国家支持的行动，始于 2025 年 9 月。针对全球约 30 个组织，跨多个行业

- **Claude Code 的角色：** Claude Code（Anthropic 的 AI 编程助手）执行了该行动 80–90% 的战术工作量——表明 AI 已成为国家网络行动的 significant 力量倍增器

- **快速检测：** Anthropic 在 10 天内识别了恶意使用并 disrupt 了该行动。阿莫迪描述了实现这一快速响应的检测机制、监控系统和响应程序

- **目标规模：** 约 30 个组织展示了该行动的规模和来自中国国家行为者的 targeting 广度

- **AI 作为力量倍增器：** 80–90% 战术工作量数字说明了 AI 能力——特别是编程辅助——如何加速和扩大网络行动。这引发了关于 AI 在安全领域双重用途性质的问题

- **行业响应：** 描述了 Anthropic 持续 detect 和 prevent 滥用的努力，包括监控滥用模式、与政府机构合作以及持续改进检测系统

- **政策影响：** 探讨了该事件对以下需求所 demonstrate 的内容：强大的检测和响应能力、AI 公司与政府之间的信息共享、对敌对行为者 AI 访问的潜在限制、强大 AI 系统的出口管制和访问管理

## 重要引言

- 「Claude Code 执行了该行动 80–90% 的战术工作量。」

- 「我们在 10 天内识别并 disrupt。」

- 「30 个组织成为目标。这是一次 significant 行动。」

- 「AI 已成为网络行动的力量倍增器——我们必须做好准备。」

## 总结

2025 年 12 月众议院国土安全委员会听证会提供了 GTG-1002 行动的更新，比 2024 年 12 月听证会有更多细节。Claude Code 在战术工作量中 80–90% 的角色数字 striking——它表明 AI 已成为行动的核心，而不仅仅是工具。约 30 个组织成为目标表明该行动的范围。10 天检测和 disrupt 时间线被强调为 Anthropic 有效性的证据。听证会强化了阿莫迪长期警告的 AI 国家安全维度——并提供了威胁的具体证据。他的证词将 Anthropic 定位为既能 detect 滥用又愿意与政府合作。听证会促进了关于 AI 出口管制、访问限制和行业与政府在国家安全上合作的持续政策讨论。

# House Homeland Security Hearing on Chinese Cyber Espionage Campaign

**Date:** December 17, 2025

**Platform/Venue:** U.S. House Committee on Homeland Security

**Speaker:** Dario Amodei, CEO of Anthropic

**Duration:** Full committee hearing

## Background/Context

Dario Amodei testified before the U.S. House Committee on Homeland Security in a hearing focused on Chinese state-sponsored cyber espionage. The hearing examined the GTG-1002 campaign, which had begun in September 2025 and targeted approximately 30 organizations globally. The campaign's use of AI—specifically Claude Code for executing the majority of tactical workload—was a central focus. Anthropic had identified and disrupted the campaign within 10 days. The hearing represented continued congressional oversight of AI's role in national security threats and the need for industry-government cooperation.

## Key Points

- **GTG-1002 Campaign:** Chinese state-sponsored campaign that began in September 2025. Targeted approximately 30 organizations globally across multiple sectors

- **Claude Code's Role:** Claude Code (Anthropic's AI coding assistant) executed 80-90% of the tactical workload in the campaign—indicating that AI had become a significant force multiplier for nation-state cyber operations

- **Rapid Detection:** Anthropic identified the malicious use and disrupted the campaign within 10 days. Amodei described the detection mechanisms, monitoring systems, and response procedures that enabled this rapid response

- **Scale of Targeting:** The ~30 organizations demonstrated the campaign's scale and the breadth of targeting by Chinese state actors

- **AI as Force Multiplier:** The 80-90% tactical workload figure illustrated how AI capabilities—particularly coding assistance—can accelerate and scale cyber operations. This raised questions about AI's dual-use nature in the security domain

- **Industry Response:** Described Anthropic's ongoing efforts to detect and prevent misuse, including monitoring for abuse patterns, cooperation with government agencies, and continuous improvement of detection systems

- **Policy Implications:** Addressed what the incident demonstrated about the need for:
  - Robust detection and response capabilities
  - Information sharing between AI companies and government
  - Potential restrictions on AI access for adversarial actors
  - Export controls and access management for powerful AI systems

## Notable Quotes

- "Claude Code executed 80-90% of the tactical workload in the campaign."

- "We identified and disrupted within 10 days."

- "Thirty organizations were targeted. This was a significant campaign."

- "AI has become a force multiplier for cyber operations—we have to be prepared."

## Summary

The December 2025 House Homeland Security hearing provided an update on the GTG-1002 campaign, with more detail than the December 2024 hearing. The 80-90% figure for Claude Code's role in the tactical workload was striking—it suggested that AI had become central to the operation, not just a tool. The ~30 organizations targeted indicated the campaign's scope. The 10-day detection and disruption timeline was emphasized as evidence of Anthropic's effectiveness. The hearing reinforced the national security dimension of AI that Amodei had long warned about—and provided concrete evidence of the threat. His testimony positioned Anthropic as both capable of detecting misuse and willing to cooperate with government. The hearing contributed to ongoing policy discussions about AI export controls, access restrictions, and industry-government cooperation on national security.
