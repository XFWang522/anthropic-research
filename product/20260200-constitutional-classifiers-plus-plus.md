- **可解释性：** 保留可解释的、基于原则的分类

# Constitutional Classifiers++

**Date:** February 2026  
**Type:** Research Paper  
**arXiv:** 2601.04603

## Description

Constitutional Classifiers++ delivered major improvements over the original Constitutional Classifiers: 40x cost reduction and 0.05% refusal rate. The research demonstrated that interpretable, principle-based safety could be scaled to production efficiency while maintaining exceptional safety—nearly eliminating inappropriate refusals while blocking harmful content.

## Technical Details

- **Cost:** **40x cost reduction** compared to original Constitutional Classifiers—production-viable economics
- **Refusal Rate:** **0.05%**—dramatically reduced false positives (unnecessary refusals of safe content)
- **Safety:** Maintained or improved harmful content blocking
- **Architecture:** Optimizations to SAE-based classifier pipeline; efficient feature computation
- **Scalability:** Suitable for high-throughput API and consumer product
- **Interpretability:** Retained interpretable, principle-based classification

## Significance & Impact

- **Production Viability:** 40x cost reduction made Constitutional Classifiers practical at scale
- **User Experience:** 0.05% refusal rate addressed over-refusal complaints
- **Safety + UX:** Proved safety and usability could improve together
- **Research Maturity:** Demonstrated evolution from research to production system
- **Industry Model:** Approach could influence safety practices across AI industry
